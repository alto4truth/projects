Each neuron processes its inputs using an activation function and passes

ANNs are particularly useful for classification tasks, as they can
approximate complex functions and recognize non-linear relationships
in data. Their simplicity and computational efficiency make them especially suitable for real-time and embedded applications, such as
intrusion detection in vehicular networks. Compared to Convolutional
Neural Networks (CNNs), which are specialized for image and spatial
data, ANNs are lightweight and require fewer computational resources.
