In addition to basic ANNs, advanced architectures such as CNNs,
Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), and Long Short-Term Memory
(LSTM) networks have also been developed to address complex data
types and temporal patterns. CNNs are particularly effective for spatial
data like images and are widely used in computer vision, but they
are computationally intensive due to their deep hierarchical structure.
While they could theoretically process CAN traffic as a 1D sequence,
their overhead is often unjustified for the relatively simpler patterns
in CAN attack detection. RNNs and LSTMs are tailored for sequential
data, capturing temporal dependencies in time-series applications such
