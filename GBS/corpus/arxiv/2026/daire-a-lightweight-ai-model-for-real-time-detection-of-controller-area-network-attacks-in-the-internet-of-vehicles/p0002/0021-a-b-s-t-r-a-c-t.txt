DL models have demonstrated superior capabilities in learning complex patterns from CAN bus data. For instance, Song et al. (2020)
proposed a deep convolutional neural network (Deep CNN) based
IDS for CAN bus networks in vehicles. It uses a reduced InceptionResNet architecture. The method utilizes raw bitwise CAN ID sequences
converted into 2D frames, enabling the model to learn sequential
patterns without requiring manual feature engineering. The model
outperformed conventional ML approaches, achieving high accuracy
and F1 score across multiple attack types, including DoS, spoofing, and
fuzzy attacks. The method processed real CAN data in near real-time
