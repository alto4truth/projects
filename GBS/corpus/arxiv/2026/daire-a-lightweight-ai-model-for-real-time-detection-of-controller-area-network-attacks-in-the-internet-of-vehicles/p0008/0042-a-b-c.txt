5.3.1. Hyperparameter tuning on CICIoV2024 subset
In the first experiment, we focused on determining the optimal hyperparameters for the DAIRE model. For this experiment, we randomly
chose 50% of the samples of each class from the CICIoV2024 dataset.
We then partitioned the dataset into 80% training and 20% testing sets.

different set of 20% for testing. The results obtained from these experiments are shown in Table 6 and visualized using a confusion matrix in
