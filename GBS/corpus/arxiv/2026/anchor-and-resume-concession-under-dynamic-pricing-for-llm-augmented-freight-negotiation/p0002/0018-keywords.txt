strategies across 12 𝑆 values spanning the full spread range,
providing statistical coverage at scale. Second, a comparison

a robustness experiment replaces the algorithmic carriers

performance holds against stochastic, language-capable counterparties. The second and third experiments are presented
together in Section 6. All loads are synthetically generated; no
