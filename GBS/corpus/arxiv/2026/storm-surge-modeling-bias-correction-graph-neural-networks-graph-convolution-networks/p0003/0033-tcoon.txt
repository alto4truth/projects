sequential LSTM-based approach (”LSTM”) [ 77] for Hurricane Idalia (2023). Gauge
stations in which StormNet (LSTM) yields better performance are denoted with purple (yellow), while larger (smaller) data point radii represent greater (lower) values of
RMSE for the considered station. Each station is represented only by the model that
yields the lowest RMSE (i.e., the best model). The gray-outlined dark blue line indicates the storm track. 24 h- and 36 h-ahead predictions are shown in (a, b) and (c, d),
respectively. Predictions for the full test dataset are shown in (a, c) while predictions
for a 2-day period around landfall are shown in (b, d).
