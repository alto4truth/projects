4 Conclusions
In this work, we introduce and explore the use of StormNet, a spatiotemporal graph
neural network model, employing convolution and attention components, for bias correction of storm surge numerical forecasts. In our proposed model, graph nodes are
used to describe gauge stations, and node connectivity is defined based on correlations
between water level observations at each gauge station. We demonstrate the performance of StormNet in predicting 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 24, 36, 48, and 72 h-ahead biases in
ADCIRC water level forecasts at 16 gauge stations along the U.S. Gulf Coast region
