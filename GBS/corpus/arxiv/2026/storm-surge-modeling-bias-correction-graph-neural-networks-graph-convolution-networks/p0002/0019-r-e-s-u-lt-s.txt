2.5.1 Experimental Details
StormNet was trained using the Adam optimizer [ 97] for 200 epochs. The initial
learning rate is set to 3e−5, and the weight decay is 5e−7 with a batch size of 20.
Regarding the past time window, by explicitly examining values in the range of 6
to 48 h, we found the lower error values were obtained with past window values in
the range of 42 to 48 hours (see Supplementary Information, Table 1). Training and
inference were performed on a single NVIDIA A100 graphical processing unit.
