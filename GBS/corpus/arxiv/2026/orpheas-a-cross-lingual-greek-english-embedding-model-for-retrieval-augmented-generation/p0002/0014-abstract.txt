3 Development of ORPHEAS
This section presents the development of ORPHEAS, a Greek–English embedding model for bilingual RAG systems.
Initially, We describe the datasets used for training, followed by the knowledge graph-based fine-tuning methodology
and finally present the model architecture and training procedure.
3.1 Datasets
The development of ORPHEAS leverages a diverse collection of corpora spanning multiple domains. Since Greek
is the primary target language and high-quality Greek resources are substantially harder to obtain than English ones,
the corpus is weighted toward Greek, encompassing encyclopedic knowledge, parliamentary discourse, legal documentation, administrative jurisprudence and social media content. English is represented through MS-MARCO, a
large-scale IR dataset for which abundant resources are readily available.
