4 Experimental Analysis
In this section, we conduct a comprehensive evaluation of the proposed ORPHEAS model for RAG application and
compare its performance against that of the most efficient and widely used embedding models: Microsoft’s Multingual
E5 [Wang et al., 2024], MPnet [Song et al., 2020], MiniLM [Reimers and Gurevych, 2019] and mGTE [Zhang et al.,
2024]. To further assess the quality of the generated fine-tuning dataset independently of the model architecture, we
include GEM-XLM-RoBERTa [Apostolopoulou et al., 2025] in our experimental analysis, a model pre-trained on a
large Greek–English corpus, which is fine-tuned following the same high-quality dataset as ORPHEAS.
