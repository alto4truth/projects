2 Related work
Text embedding models constitute of primary importance in many industrial and scientific applications, enabling
advances in semantic search [Reimers and Gurevych, 2019], information retrieval [Bajaj et al., 2018] and RetrievalAugmented Generation [Lewis et al., 2021].
