3.2 Knowledge Graph-Based Fine-Tuning Methodology
For developing ORPHEAS, we adopted the knowledge graph-based fine-tuning methodology proposed by Koursaris
et al. [2026]. This approach leverages structured knowledge extraction to generate semantically enriched training data
through contrastive learning. In detail, the methodology employs a domain-specific ontologyO, which structures
the knowledge graph around five core concepts: DOCUMENT, CHUNK, ENTITY, QUESTIONand ATOMIC_FACT.
