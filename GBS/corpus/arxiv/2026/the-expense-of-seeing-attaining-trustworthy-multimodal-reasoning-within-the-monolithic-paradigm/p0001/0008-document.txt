2 Challenging Existing Assumptions: The Crisis
in Evaluation
To build trustworthy data science systems, our evaluation metrics
must rigorously isolate thesourceof a model’s predictive power. The
standard approach of evaluating multimodal capabilities currently
relies heavily on the paradigm of data ablation.
