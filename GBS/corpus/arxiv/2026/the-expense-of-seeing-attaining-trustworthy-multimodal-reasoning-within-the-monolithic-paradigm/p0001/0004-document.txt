1 The Illusion of Multimodal Synthesis
The trajectory of Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD)
has reached a critical inflection point. We are no longer merely
mining tabular databases, massive graphs or isolated text corpora;
the frontier of Modern AI and Big Data is the construction of unified “world models” [12]. These systems are expected to ingest
and seamlessly synthesise disparate, high-dimensional information from text, images, videos, and complex topological graphs
etc. to perform faithful, cross-domain decision-making. At the core
of this frontier sits the Vision-Language Model (VLM), predominantly governed by the monolithic Vision Encoder-Projector-LLM
architectural paradigm [1, 2, 8, 11].
