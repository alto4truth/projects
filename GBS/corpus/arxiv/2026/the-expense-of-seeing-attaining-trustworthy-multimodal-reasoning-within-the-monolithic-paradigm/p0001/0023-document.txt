7 A Roadmap for KDD: From a Diagnostic
Constraint to SSC-Guided Architectures
The introduction of the Semantic Sufficiency Criterion (SSC) represents a seismic paradigm shift that uniquely targets the core
mandate of the KDD community. Historically, the field has relied
ondata scavenging, i.e., scraping massive, noisy repositories of
loosely correlated image-text pairs or even the manually intensive image-text pair creation, and relying on the false hope that
blind, billion-parameter next token prediction will force emergent
alignment.
