Metrics and error taxonomy.We report the results along the following two metrics:
• Accuracy.Exact-match agreement with the FLAMA oracle,
broken down by AO, blueprint, and model family (generalpurpose vs. reasoning-optimized).
• Cost.End-to-endruntime(in seconds) andtoken usage
(prompt+completion).
Errors are categorized into four mutually exclusivefailure modes
aligned with our analysis: (i)Unparseable(violates XML contract),
(ii)Format-correct but wrong(includessemantic slipssuch as misreading alternative vs. mandatory), (iii)Partial/Truncated(incomplete outputs due to context/output limits), and (iv)Hallucinated
elements(items not in the blueprint).
