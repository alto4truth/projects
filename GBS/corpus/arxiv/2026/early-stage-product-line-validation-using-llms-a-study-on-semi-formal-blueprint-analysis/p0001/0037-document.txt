6 Discussion
Where LLMs fit in early scoping.Our results support a pragmatic
placement of LLMs at theearlieststage, when blueprints are semiformal and solver-ready model is not yet available. In this context,
reasoning-oriented models provideverification-stylefeedback with
near-solver accuracy (e.g., forAO10,AO11,AO16), quick structural
sanity checks (e.g., forAO1,AO3,AO6,AO9), and concise explanations
that help stakeholders refine scope decisions before formalization.
Accuracy and explainability over speed.Because early validation
is iterative and not latency-critical, we prioritize correctness and
interpretability over raw speed. The observed runtime/token overheads are acceptable in practice given the benefit of solver-like
guidancewithoutrequiring a formal model. Reasoning-optimized
