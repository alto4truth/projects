This uniform pipeline ensures consistent results across models

Inference.Each triple (including model, blueprint, and AO) is executed once with deterministic decoding (temperature=0, greedy)
via the provider’s public API only, i.e., no tool use, no solver calls,
no post-processing. We record request/response payloads, the raw
XML, the rationale, and wall-clock runtime. Besides, timeouts and
truncations are kept (and evaluated) as produced.
