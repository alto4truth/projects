Semantic fidelity evaluates the degree to which model-generated responses
preserve the conceptual content expressed by clinicians. We operationalize this
dimension using cosine similarity computed over embeddings produced by the
BioBERT-mnli-snli-scinli-scitail-mednli-stsbencoder. Because this metric
does not capture fine-grained factual inaccuracies (e.g., incorrect dosages or omitted
clinical entities), we interpret it as a measure ofconceptual fidelity, supplemented by
domain-specific analyses reported in Section 5.
