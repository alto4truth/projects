MedQuAD Subset Construction
Due to the high inference cost of commercial models, evaluation was conducted on
a reduced subset of 50 MedQuAD questions. This subset reflects explicit budget
constraints and follows a structured selection protocol designed to preserve linguistic diversity. The experiment is framed as a controlled cross-architecture comparison
rather than a population-scale benchmark.
