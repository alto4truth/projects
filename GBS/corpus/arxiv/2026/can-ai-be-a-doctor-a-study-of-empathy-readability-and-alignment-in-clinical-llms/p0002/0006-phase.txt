For this study, we selected a subset of 16,400 QA pairs covering 37 question types.
We excluded three sections of the original corpus: A.D.A.M. Medical Encyclopedia,
MedlinePlus Drugs, and MedlinePlus Herbal Supplements. These sections account
for approximately 31,000 entries. They follow editorial standards that differ from the
core NIH sources and show substantial variation in writing style, clinical depth, and
structure. Their exclusion ensures consistency in tone, source reliability, and clinical
framing. This controlled subset enables fair comparisons across models.
