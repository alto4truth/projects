3.3. Integrated Data Flow and Architecture Benefits
The seven modules operate in sequence, with each stage editing or adding
to the data stream:
1) Route selection (Map Display Handler)
2) Route feature extraction and structuring (Route Processor)
3) Rule-based reference velocity profile generation (Velocity Profile Generator)
4) Vehicle dynamics simulation with realistic constraints (PID Controller)
5) Personalized, route context informed velocity and acceleration prediction (Driver LSTM Module)
6) Physics-based energy consumption estimation (BEV Energy Consumption Model)
7) Comprehensive visualization and validation (Visualization Module)
This modular architecture directly addresses the identified research gaps
by seamlessly integrating driver-specific behavioral modeling with comprehensive map context in a computationally efficient framework.
