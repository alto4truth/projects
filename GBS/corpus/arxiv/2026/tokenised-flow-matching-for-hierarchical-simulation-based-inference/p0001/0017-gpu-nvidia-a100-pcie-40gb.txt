A.3 Benchmark task specifications
We specify the six hierarchical SBI benchmark tasks summarised in Table 1. Each task was adapted
from the non-hierarchical SBI benchmark (Lueckmann et al., 2021) under one of two hierarchical
structures:separated, where the original parameters split naturally into global and site-level groups
(Gaussian Linear, Gaussian Linear Uniform, SIR, SLCP); andpartial pooling, where new global
hyperparameters govern the distribution of independently replicated local parameters across sites
(Gaussian Mixture, Two Moons). Table 6 lists the simulator and prior distributions for every task.
