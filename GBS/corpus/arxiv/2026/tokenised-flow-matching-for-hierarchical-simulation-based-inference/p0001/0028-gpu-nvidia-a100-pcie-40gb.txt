A.10ℓ-C2ST Evaluation Protocol
We evaluate posterior quality using the local classifier two-sample test (ℓ-C2ST) (Linhart et al.,
2023), which diagnoses whether samples from the approximate posteriorqϕ(θ|x )are distinguishable
from samples drawn from the jointp(θ, x)conditioned on the same observation.
Classifier architecture.We use fully-connected neural networks with two hidden layers of 32
units and ReLU activations. Each classifierdψ : Rdx+dθ → [0, 1]is trained using the Adam optimiser
with learning rate3 × 10−4, a batch size of 100, and early stopping with patience of 100 epochs and
a minimum improvement threshold of10−2 on a 90/10 train/validation split, for a maximum of 1000
epochs.
