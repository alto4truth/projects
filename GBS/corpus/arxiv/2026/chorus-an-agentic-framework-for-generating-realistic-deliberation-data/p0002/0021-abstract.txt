4 Use Case Scenario: Integration with DELIBERATE
To validate CHORUSin a realistic deployment setting, we integrated the framework with DELIBERATE, an AI-powered
web platform for facilitating citizen engagement on policy issues through structured online discussions. DELIBERATE
employs NLP-based pipelines to extract thematic trends, identify consensus and contention and generate deliberation
reports from user contributions [Livieris et al., 2026], supporting evidence-based policymaking through synthesized
stakeholder insights. This section describes the pilot deployment configuration, presents the generated discussion
outputs and reports the results of an expert evaluation assessing content realism, discussion coherence and analytical
utility.
