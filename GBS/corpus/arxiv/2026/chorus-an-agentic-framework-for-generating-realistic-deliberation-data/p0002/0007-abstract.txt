• We develop an agentic simulation framework named CHORUS, which orchestrates a cast of LLM-powered
actors with clearly defined, behaviorally consistent personas to generate realistic deliberation discussions on
interactive web platforms.
• We propose a Poisson process-based temporal model governing actor participation dynamics, alongside a
structured tool suite enabling seamless integration with external web platforms through the translation of
generative outputs into concrete platform actions.
