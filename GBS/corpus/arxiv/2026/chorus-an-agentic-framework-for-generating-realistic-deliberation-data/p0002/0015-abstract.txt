Algorithm 1 presents the main simulation cycle governing the execution of the agentic deliberation framework. A key
design decision concerns the mechanism governing when each actor participates in the discussion. A persona-driven
approach, wherein the agent itself decides whether to post or act based on its persona and the current discussion state,
was deliberately avoided, as it introduces systematic bias: the LLM’s intrinsic tendencies would disproportionately
shape participation timing, resulting in engagement patterns that reflect model priors rather than realistic user behavior
