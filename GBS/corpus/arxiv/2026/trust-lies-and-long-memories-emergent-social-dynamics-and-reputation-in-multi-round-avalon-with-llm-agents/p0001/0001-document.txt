(Alice, Bob, Charlie, Diana, Eve) play repeated Avalon games with randomized roles. After each game, agents generate
self-reflections and observations about other players, which persist into subsequent games as memory. This cross-game
memory enables reputation formation, with agents referencing past behavior in their strategic reasoning (example quotes
shown on right).
