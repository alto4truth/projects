3.3 Team Composition
The ratio of good to evil players varies with player count.
In the 5-player variant used in most of our experiments, the
Good Team consists of Merlin and 2 Loyal Servants, while
the Evil Team consists of the Assassin and 1 generic Minion.
Role configurations for other player counts (6–10 players),
which introduce additional special roles like Percival, Morgana, Mordred, and Oberon, are provided in Section B.
