5.4 Meta-Strategic Adaptation
As reputations form, a strategic tension emerges: relying
on past behavior to predict future actions can be exploited
by adversaries who recognize this pattern. We examined
whether agents develop awareness of this meta-level dynamic
and adapt accordingly.
