4.4 Experimental Conditions
We collected 188 games organized into four disjoint datasets,
summarized in Table 2.
Table 2: Dataset overview.
Dataset Games N Mem Reas.
A: Reputation 50 5 Full Low
B: Count (Mem) 60 5–10 Full Low
C: Count (No Mem) 60 5–10 None Low
D: Reasoning 18 5 Full L/M/H
Total 188
Dataset A enables deep analysis of reputation formation
over 50 repeated games with the same 5 agents. Datasets B
and C form matched pairs for isolating memory effects across
player counts. Dataset D varies reasoning depth to explore
how computational budget affects agent behavior.
