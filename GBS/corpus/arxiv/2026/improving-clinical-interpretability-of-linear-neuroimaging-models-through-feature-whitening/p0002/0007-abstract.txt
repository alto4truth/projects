2. MATERIALS AND METHODS
2.1. Datasets
Classification tasks for BD and SCZ were conducted using
aggregated multi-site neuroimaging datasets. For BD, we
used the BIOBD and BSNIP datasets [11, 12], comprising
861 participants across 12 acquisition sites. Among them,
56.4% are female, 44.1% were diagnosed with BD, and the
mean age is37.69±11.75years. For SCZ, classification was
performed using the SCHIZCONNECT-VIP dataset [13], totaling 604 participants across 4 acquisition sites. 38.2% of
its participants are female, 45.5% were diagnosed with SCZ,
and the mean age is33.33±12.34years.
