In this work, we leverage correlation-based zero-phase
component analysis (ZCA-cor) whitening to enhance the interpretability of linear models trained on region-of-interest
(ROI)-based brain features. ZCA-cor whitening has been
identified as an optimal procedure for generating sphered
variables that closely preserve the structure of the original
data [10]. We implemented both ZCA-cor, and an original
regularized ZCA-cor as custom transformers compatible with
