Analysis:This text uses correct French words and localized syntax, but the meaning is total nonsense (e.g., “a bottle of life in the”). The monolingualHQmodel was fooled by the surface-level
fluency, but theMLmodel correctly identified it as noise. This indicates that multilingual embeddings help the model verify semantic coherence, as “nonsense” rarely aligns well across different
languages in latent space. In Figure C.2 we see that this behaviour generalizes to Spanish in much
less pronounced way, but does not hold for Chinese.
