that semantically equivalent words occupy similar topological positions regardless of surface form.
For example, the English word “Science” and the German word “Wissenschaft” are positioned near
each other in the 768-dimensional embedding space, as both relate similarly to concepts like “logic”
and “fact.” This alignment enables classification based on geometric position in latent space rather
than surface-level syntax.
