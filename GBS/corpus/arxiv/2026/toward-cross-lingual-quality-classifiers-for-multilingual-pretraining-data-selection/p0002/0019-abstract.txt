Tables 1 and 2 evaluate the effect of multilingual quality filtering on downstream performance for a
1B model in Chinese and Spanish. The multilingual classifier (ML) achieves the highest aggregate
accuracy and lowest average rank in both languages, outperforming both No filtering and monolingual high-quality filtering (HQ).
Improvements are observed consistently across diverse reasoning and natural language understanding benchmarks, such as ARC Clark et al. (2018), GMMLU Singh et al. (2025), XNLI (Conneau
et al., 2018), and Include (Romanou et al., 2024). While HQ occasionally yields marginal gains on
