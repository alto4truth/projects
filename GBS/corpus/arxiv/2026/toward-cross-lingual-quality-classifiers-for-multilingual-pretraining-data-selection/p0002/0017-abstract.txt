Multilingual Synergy.We first investigate whether data quality is a general feature shared across
samples from different languages, or if it is language-specific. Our hypothesis is that there exist
shared regularities in XLM-RoBERTa’s embedding space that correlate with quality. If we train a
model to detect these regularities across multiple languages, then we would obtain a general classifier that could recognize high-quality samples in unseen languages, and even boost the data quality
for languages it was trained on. In order to test this hypothesis, we train a general multilingual
