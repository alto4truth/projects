While model-based filtering has proven effective for high-resource languages, the multilingual domain faces a critical challenge: many languages lack sufficient native high-quality data to train
effective standalone classifiers. This work investigates whether quality classifiers can generalize
across languages by exploiting shared semantic structures in multilingual embedding spaces, which
would enable high-resource languages to effectively support filtering for low-resource ones.
