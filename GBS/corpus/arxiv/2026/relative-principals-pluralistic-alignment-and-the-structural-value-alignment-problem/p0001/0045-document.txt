The structural account also motivates the followingscaling hypothesisin the context of value alignment.
Scaling Hypothesis for Value-Aligned AI.
As AI systems increase in (i) model generality, (ii) deployment scope, and (iii) stakeholder diversity,
the difficulty of achieving value alignment increases because these forms of scaling systematically
amplify informational asymmetries between humans and AI systems and pluralistic conflicts among
the principals whose values the system must serve.
