3 The Three Axes of Value Alignment
Having defined value alignment structurally, we can now examine the three axes along which instances of
misalignment arise: the objectives axis, the information axis, and the principals axis. Each axis represents a
distinct yet interrelated source of divergence between the intentions of human principals and the behaviour
of artificial agents. Understanding these axes in detail clarifies why alignment cannot be reduced to a single
technical or normative question; instead, it must be treated as a multi-dimensional problem emerging from the
socio-technical structure of human-AI interaction.
