5 Alignment As Ongoing Governance

moral challenge but a class of structurally related problems arising wherever a principal delegates a task to an
agent. In human-human contexts, such delegation introduces value misalignment because principals and agents
already possess distinct and sometimes competing values. In contrast, artificial agents do not possess intrinsic
