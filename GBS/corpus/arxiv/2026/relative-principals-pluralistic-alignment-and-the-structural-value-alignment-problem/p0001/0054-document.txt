their design, objectives, and governance, which are made primarily by shareholders (developers, corporations,
and regulators). Alignment, therefore, requires rebalancing the power dynamics that shape AI development,
ensuring that diverse voices have meaningful influence in defining and evaluating alignment objectives [ 57].
