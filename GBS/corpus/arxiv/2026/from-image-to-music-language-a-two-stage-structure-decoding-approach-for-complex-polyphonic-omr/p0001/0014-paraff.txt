B Algorithm Summary
This appendix provides a self-contained walkthrough of the BeadSolver topology-recognition loop.
The goal is to make the design intent clear: why the loop is structured the way it is, what each
sub-step decides, and how the evaluation function keeps the search honest. Readers who want
implementation details can cross-reference Appendix A; those interested in the experimental setup
should see Section 5.
