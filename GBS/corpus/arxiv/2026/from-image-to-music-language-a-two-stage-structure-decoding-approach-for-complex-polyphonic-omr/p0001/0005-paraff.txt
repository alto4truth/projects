5.6 Ablations and Qualitative Examples
We report three ablations that remove one component at a time.
Replace learned picker by rule-based picker.We replace BeadPicker with a purely rule-based
picker and keep the same search procedure. This isolates the effect of learned guidance in the solver.
Without the learned picker, search alone provides only limited gains over greedy and leaves many
measures unusable (see Table 8).
