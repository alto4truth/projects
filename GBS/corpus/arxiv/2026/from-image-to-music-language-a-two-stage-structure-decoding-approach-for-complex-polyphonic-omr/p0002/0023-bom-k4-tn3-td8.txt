E Symbol Semantic Type Inventory
See Table 16 for the label definitions used by the semantic model. In Starry, semantic labels fall into
two basic geometric kinds: point labels and vertical-line labels. In the training annotations, both are
marked in red on the score image and used directly as supervision targets for the semantic model.
The table gives the full inventory of these labels together with a representative glyph for each type.
For readability, the labels are grouped by musical role; theContextualgroup covers helper tokens
that carry pitch-position or rendering metadata rather than independent note events.
