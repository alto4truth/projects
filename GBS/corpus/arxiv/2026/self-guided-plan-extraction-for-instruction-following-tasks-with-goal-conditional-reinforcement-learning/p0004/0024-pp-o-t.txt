6 Ablation Study
6.1 Component Interaction Ablation
To quantify the contribution of each module of
the SuperIgor framework, we conduct an ablation
study in which individual components are removed
from the training pipeline. We evaluate the influence of four factors: (1) Ontology-Based Training
Plan Generation, (2) Curriculum design in the RL
stage, (3) LLM plan-model pretraining (SFT), and
(4) DPO finetuning based on RL agent performance
