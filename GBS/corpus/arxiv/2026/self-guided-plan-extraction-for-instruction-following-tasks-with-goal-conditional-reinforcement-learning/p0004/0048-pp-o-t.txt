We observe a clear relationship between ontology quality and downstream performance. As
shown in Figure 7, models with higher coverage
recover a larger fraction of the reference subtask
set. At the same time, Table 4 shows that lowperforming models tend to exhibit higher oversampling and a larger number of missing and spurious
subtasks.
