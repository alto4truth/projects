After constructing the ontology G= (V, E) ,
each goal plan P[I] is expanded with its dependencies. For every subtask s∈ P[I] , we recursively
collect all prerequisites in G. The union of these
subtasks with the original goals defines the plan’s
vertices, which are then topologically sorted so
that prerequisites precede dependents. The result
is a linearized plan P containing the goals and all
supporting subtasks (Algorithm 4).
