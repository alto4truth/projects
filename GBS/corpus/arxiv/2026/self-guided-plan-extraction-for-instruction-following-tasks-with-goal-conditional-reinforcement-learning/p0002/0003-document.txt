The training process proceeds through the following stages:
1. Plan Generation(4.1): The LLM extracts
possible subtasks from instructions and generates multiple candidate plans in natural language during the initial cycle (Cycle 1). In
subsequent cycles (Cycle 2–N), the candidate
pool is iteratively refined by filtering and reprioritization, based on how well the plans
align with the RL agent’s performance.
2. Policy Learning(4.2): The RL agent is
trained to execute the selected plans in the
environment.
3. Plan Validation(4.3): The quality of candidate plans is evaluated according to the RL
agent’s success rate and execution trajectories.
4. LLM Fine-Tuning(4.4): The language
model is fine-tuned with feedback derived
from validation, aligning its scoring of plans
with the agent’s actual performance.
