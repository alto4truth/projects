To evaluate generalization, FOCUS employs two
distinct test sets. TheParaphrasesset consists
of Combo instructions from the training set reformulated with novel vocabulary and syntax. The
New Objectsset introduces new combinations of

never occurred together in a single instruction. This
structure allows FOCUS to assess both robustness
