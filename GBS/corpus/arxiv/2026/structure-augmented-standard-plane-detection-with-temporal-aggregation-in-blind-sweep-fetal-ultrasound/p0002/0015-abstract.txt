3.2. Quantitative Evaluation
Metrics.We reportPrecision (P),Recall (R), andF1on
frame-wise labels. Timing fidelity is evaluated by: (i)absolute time error, which measures temporal misalignment as the
mean of|t pred −t gt|between the predicted and GT keyframes,
which is sensitive to isolated false peaks; and (ii)keyframe
number error, which is the discrepancy of lengths between
the predicted and GT segments.
