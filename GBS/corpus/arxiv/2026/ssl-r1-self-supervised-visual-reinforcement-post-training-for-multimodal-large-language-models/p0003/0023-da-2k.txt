Training Dynamics.Figure 4 shows how our SSL-R1
evolves during training. Generally, the rewards gradually
increase with the training steps, while different tasks exhibit
different trends. Specifically, Visual Similarity and Rotation Prediction rapidly increase in early training stages and
finally converge to near-perfect accuracies. Region Inpainting has a lower reward initially and increases gradually at a
steady speed. Geometric Correspondence and Patch Ordering have the lowest initial rewards and evolve slowly at the
beginning, while the growth rates get faster in later stages.
For multi-task post-training, adding more tasks generally
leads to increased rewards, likely because multiple tasks
synergize and make the overall learning process easier.
