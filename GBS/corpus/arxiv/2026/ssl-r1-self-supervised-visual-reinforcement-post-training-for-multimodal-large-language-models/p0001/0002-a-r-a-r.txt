3. Methodology
Our SSL-R1 is a generic self-supervised RL post-training
framework. As shown in Fig. 2, it includes five selfsupervised tasks that target different aspects of visual information, providing comprehensive coverage of visioncentric reasoning capabilities. In this section, we first introduce related RL preliminaries in Sec. 3.1. We then detail
our SSL-R1 pipeline including task and reward designs in
Sec. 3.2.

Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards.

maintaining strong alignment with the inherent correctness
