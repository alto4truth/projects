The language distribution in the stuttered-speech technology literature is highly skewed towards English with 152 papers focusing on English. In contrast, the next four most common languages are German (22 papers), Mandarin (17 papers),
Hindi (5 papers), and Marathi (5 papers). All other languages in
our sample are covered by three or fewer papers (see Figure 3).
