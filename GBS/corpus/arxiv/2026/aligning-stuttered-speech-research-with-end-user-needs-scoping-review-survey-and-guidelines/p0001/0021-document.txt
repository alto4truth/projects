2.3.4. Discussion
Overall, our findings indicate that the stuttered-speech technologies field is active, but narrowly scoped. The literature is
overwhelmingly organized around monolingual stutter classification tasks, with inconsistent task naming and an implicit default toward intended speech recognition. Stakeholder collaboration and open-science practices are still the exception rather
than the rule, and work that investigates ethics, explainability,
