as follows. Section 2 maps the current research landscape for

needs related to speech technology. In Section 5, we bring these
strands together by contrasting research with stakeholder priorities and highlighting systematic gaps and design implications.
Finally, Section 6 outlines our actionable recommendations for
future research.
