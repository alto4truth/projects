Research-Focused Gaps.Within the research-focused portion
of the literature, we found only a handful of papers on explainable methods, ethics or deployment, compared to a larger body
of work on building stutter identification or ASR models. Most
systems report only global metrics, with little support for clinicians to inspect or interrogate individual predictions. From
theSLPsperspective, explainability is non-negotiable. Understanding how reliable the model is in specific contexts, and how
outputs should inform clinical reasoning is essential and considered non-trivial.
