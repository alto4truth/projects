Speech Recognition Gaps.Our taxonomy differentiatesintendedandverbatimASR, and the stakeholder surveys indicate
that these two forms serve distinct use-cases:PWSprefer intended transcripts for communication, whileSLPsrely on verbatim transcripts for clinical work. Yet very few ASR papers
for atypical speech clearly state which transcription objective
they optimise, or which reference transcript is treated as ground
truth. This ambiguity arises becauserealstuttered-speech resources often provide only a single reference transcript (typically intended), especially for reading scenarios, whereas synthetic sources often include both verbatim and intended references.
