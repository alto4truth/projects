6.1. Research Directions
1.Diversify and Standardise: Future work should move beyond classification tasks to situation-aware modelling of stuttering, including detection tasks, models that capture variability across situations, interlocutors and languages, and
tools for longitudinal severity tracking. Researchers should
also adopt standardised terminology for tasks and annotations, such as our proposed taxonomy, to improve research
communication, benchmarks, and real-world relevance.
2.Explicate Objectives: ASR research on disfluent or stuttered
speech should clearly state whether it targets intended or verbatim transcription, and design systems capable of producing both forms. Dataset creators can help by documenting
transcription conventions and providing parallel intended and
verbatim annotations where feasible, and clearly specify the
intended use cases of such annotations.
