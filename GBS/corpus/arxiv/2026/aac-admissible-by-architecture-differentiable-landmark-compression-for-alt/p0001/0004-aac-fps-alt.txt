Hyperparameter and initialization robustness for training drift.Table 20 reports a
hyperparameter-and-initialization sweep on the SBMB=32cell described at the end of Section 5.9. The
take-away is that the training drift documented above is robust across the (learning rate, batch size,K0)
hyperparameter grid we test, but vanishes when the compressor is initialized at the architectural ceiling
(identity-on-first-minit), so the binding constraint is the default block-sparse initialization rather than a
fundamental optimization barrier on this cell.
C Ablation Studies
We conduct three targeted ablation experiments on the OSMnx city graphs (Modena and Manhattan) to
isolate the contributions of AAC’s key design choices. All experiments useK0=64teacher landmarks, seeds
{42,123,456,789,1024}, and 100 random query pairs per configuration.
