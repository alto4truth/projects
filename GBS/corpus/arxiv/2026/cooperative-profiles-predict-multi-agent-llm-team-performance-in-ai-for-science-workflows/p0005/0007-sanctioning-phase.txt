B.4.4 Collective Risk (Threshold Public Goods)
Standard:
Collective-Risk Social Dilemma (Climate Game).
Over 10 rounds, all 10 players must collectively contribute at least 100
tokens to avoid catastrophe.
Each round, you have 10 tokens and decide how many to contribute.
Tokens you don’t contribute are saved.
At the end of round 10:
- If the group total meets the threshold (100), everyone keeps their
savings.
- If not, there is a 50% chance everyone loses ALL their savings.
The challenge: contribute enough collectively without sacrificing too much
individually.
