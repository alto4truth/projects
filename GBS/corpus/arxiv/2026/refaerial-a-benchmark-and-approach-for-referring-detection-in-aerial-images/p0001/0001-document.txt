Abstract
Referring detection refers to locate the target referred
by natural languages, which has recently attracted growing research interests. However, existing datasets are limited to ground images with large object centered in relative small scenes. This paper introduces a large-scale
challenging dataset for referring detection in aerial images, termed as RefAerial. It distinguishes from conventional ground referring detection datasets by 4 characteristics: (1) low but diverse object-to-scene ratios, (2)
numerous targets
