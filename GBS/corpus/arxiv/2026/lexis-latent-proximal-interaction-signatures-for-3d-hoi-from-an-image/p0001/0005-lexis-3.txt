2.2 3D HOI Reconstruction from a Single Image
Human-only Reconstruction:Optimization methods fit a parametric 3D
body model [35,49,83] to image cues [5,35]. Regression-based methods estimate
parameters of such models [17,37,40,56,87] or a non-parametric mesh [12,42,65].
