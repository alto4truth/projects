In summary, here we make the following contributions:
1. Wegobeyondsparse,binarycontactbyinferringdense, continuousInterFields
that encodeproximitycues across entire body and object surfaces.
2. We learn the novel LEXISdictionarythat encodes action- and object-aware
latent interaction signatures, which are learned from 3D interaction datasets.
3. We develop LEXIS-Flow, a model that exploits LEXIS to estimate a 3D
human, object, and their InterFields, from an image. Notably, LEXIS-based
InterFields guide the Flow-Matching generative sampling to refine estimates
and improve the 3D HOI plausibility, without post-hoc optimization.
