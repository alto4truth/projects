3 Methodology
Overview:Given an RGB image depicting a human–object interaction
(HOI),I ∈R H×W×3, and a 3D object shape estimation by an off-the-shelf
model, the goal is to estimate the HOI in 3D, namely to estimate a 3D human
mesh (SMPL+H1 body [64]) and object mesh, shaped and posed such that they
interactrealisticallyandmatchimagecues.Technically,thisestimatestwostates:
