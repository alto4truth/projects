The Natural lexicon employs familiar, semantically meaningful English keywords to represent
operations and control flow.
start: stmt+
action_stmt: DO action END
...
DO: "do"; END: "stop";
LOOP: "repeat"; TIMES: "times";
IF: "when"; THEN: "next";
...
The Alien lexicon replaces these familiar terms
with randomized, opaque tokens. This configuration forces the model to rely exclusively on the
provided EBNF specification for symbolic induction, as the semantic cues are entirely stripped.
start: stmt+
action_stmt: DO action END
...
DO: "v_qnuc"; END: "v_ojrp";
LOOP: "v_takv"; TIMES: "v_gqge";
IF: "v_abgh"; THEN: "v_xhfm";
...
