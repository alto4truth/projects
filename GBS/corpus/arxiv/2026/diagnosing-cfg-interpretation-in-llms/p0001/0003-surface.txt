2 Preliminaries
2.1 ROBOGRIDEnvironment
ROBOGRIDis a deterministic grid-world designed
to decouple a LLM’s understanding of task logic
from its adherence to formal syntax. In this environment, a robot navigates the grid to pick up
objects which are then stored in its inventory. The
robot maintains a state (x,y,facing,inventory) ,
representing its 2D coordinates, orientation, and
the collection of held items respectively. The instruction set is as follows:
Move(dir, n) #forward/backward n steps
Turn(dir) #left/right 90 deg
Grab(item) #pick up item|key|box
Loop(n, body) #repeat body n times
If(cond, then, else?) #conditional branch
Holding(item) #state-query predicates
