B. Priors
For the operational scenario detection, a categorical
prior is required. As heat exchangers operate normally
moreoftenthantheyexperiencefailures, weassignhigher
prior probability to the no-failure mode:p(failure mode)
= [0.4; 0.2; 0.2; 0.2] (none, fouling, leakage, both). We
note that the scenario where both failure mechanisms are
active, is a very unlikely situation: albeit we still consider
it in defining the categorical prior, we do not generate
data with both leakage and fouling and consider it only
as a confounding scenario.
