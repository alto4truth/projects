Evaluation details.We evaluate model performance using the LLM-as-a-judge framework [Zheng et al., 2023]
with GPT-4o [Hurst et al., 2024]. For each test prompt, we identify agolden responsefrom the UltraFeedback pool,
defined as the response with the highest cumulative score across all four objectives. We then sample responses from our
fine-tuned models and conduct pairwise comparisons against these golden responses. The judge evaluates both overall
quality and per-objective performance, outputting a win, tie, or loss. We report theNet Win Rate(Win % − Loss %)
following Ning et al. [2023], and use paired bootstrap resampling [Koehn, 2004] with α= 0.05 to test for significant
