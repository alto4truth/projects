4 Results and Discussion
We present the experimental results for the Gemma-2-2b-it and Qwen2.5-0.5B-Instruct models. We focus on two key
dimensions:per-objective performance, measured by the win/tie/loss rate against the golden responses, andoverall
quality, measured by the improvement in net win rate over the starting REFERENCEmodel.
Detailed tabular results are provided in Section A.3, and sample responses from the resulting models can be found
in Section A.4.
