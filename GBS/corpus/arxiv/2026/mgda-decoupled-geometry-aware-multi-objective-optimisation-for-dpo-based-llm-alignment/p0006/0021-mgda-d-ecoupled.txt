6 Conclusion
In this work, we introduced MGDA-DECOUPLED, a novel geometry-aware algorithm for multi-objective LLM
alignment designed to ensure simultaneous improvement across all objectives while remaining sensitive to their
respective convergence dynamics. We systematically evaluated our proposed algorithm against other lightweight, rewardmodel-free algorithms and benchmarked strategies with varying levels of adaptivity, from fixed linear scalarisation to
dynamic schemes based on loss values and gradient geometry.
