A parallel line of research focuses on steerability, aiming to learn a set of policies or a single conditional policy
that is capable of covering relevant regions of the objective space to suit diverse user preferences [Zhong et al., 2024,
Gupta et al., 2025]. These methods typically rely on inference-time prompts or vectors to modulate model behaviour
[Yang et al., 2024b, Wang et al., 2024]. Other approaches include model averaging, where models trained on separate
objectives are fused in weight space [Ramé et al., 2023]. Similarly, Lin et al. [2024] propose interpolating the fine-tuned
