Intersectionality is a framework introduced by [Crenshaw 1989] to understand how multiple forms of oppression
interact to create distinct experiences of marginalization. In the context of AI fairness, this means that the combined
effect of multiple sensitive attributes such as race and gender can produce discriminatory outcomes that neither attribute
alone would reveal, making intersectional evaluation essential for a complete understanding of model bias.
