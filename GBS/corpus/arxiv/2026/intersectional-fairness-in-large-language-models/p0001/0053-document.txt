across subgroups, this should be interpreted with caution as SF is computed solely from model outputs and may
be affected by the limited number of non-‘unknown’ predictions. DF, however, reveals a more nuanced picture. On
Race_SES dataset, it ranges from 0.36 for Llama-3 to 1.11 for Gemini-2.0-Flash, indicating measurable inter-subgroup
disparity in favorable-outcome rates. On Race_Gender dataset, Llama-3 records the highest finite DF value of 2.37,
