Limitations of Prior Empirical studies.As shown in Table 1, prior work has evaluated LLM fairness across diverse
settings, often under zero- or few-shot prompting and with different approaches to consistency assessment. Many

adaptations such as SB-bench [Narnaware et al. 2025], CBBQ [Huang and Xiong 2024], and KoBBQ [Jin et al. 2024].
