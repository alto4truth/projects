While BBQ primarily relied on accuracy and bias scores, we additionally consider subgroup fairness measures (SF
and DF), which capture how model outputs are distributed across intersectional groups. Although these metrics are
not always fully informative, particularly in ambiguous contexts due to outcome sparsity, they reveal cases where
directional bias alone does not capture uneven subgroup treatment.
