3 Related Work
Fairness testing can be conducted at many stages of the AI lifecycle, covering the training data (e.g., detecting selection
or label bias), the ML program (e.g., testing data processing or compression algorithms), the model, and the framework
(libraries) [Chen et al. 2022]. Research has largely focused on model testing, as it is the most accessible stage for users of
pre-trained models. Some common approaches involve coreference resolution, masked language modeling, sentiment
analysis, and paired sentences [Soremekun et al. 2022].
