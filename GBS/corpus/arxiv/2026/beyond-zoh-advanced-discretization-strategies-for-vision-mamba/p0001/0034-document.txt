5.2 Inference Speed
Tables 3 and 4 show our benchmarked inference speeds across batch sizes to
evaluate the efficiency of each advanced discretization method. BIL establishes
an improvement in efficiency, outperforming the other methods in both lowlatency (with batch size 1) and high-throughput comparisons (with batch size
512). As shown in Table 4, BIL achieves the lowest latency of 13.46 ms/img at
batch size 1, making it the optimal choice for real-time applications. At batch
size 512, it maximizes GPU saturation with a peak throughput of 4,804 img/s.
While POL and FOH offer marginal gains at intermediate batch sizes (4–256),
BIL provides the most robust solution for scalable deployment.
