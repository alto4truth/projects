Unlike FOH, which can "blur" high-frequency content through linear approximation, BIL preserves fine spectral variations while maintaining system stability
and accurate frequency-domain behavior [3,8,21], making it particularly effective for ViM layers sensitive to fine spectral variations in texture and edges.

curvature in the input, resulting in smoother and more accurate latent state
updates compared to lower order hold methods [7] [24] [2]. Fig.3 shows the block
diagram that allows the discrete model to approximate smooth, non-linear transitions between samples. The discrete equivalent of the state-space system is
