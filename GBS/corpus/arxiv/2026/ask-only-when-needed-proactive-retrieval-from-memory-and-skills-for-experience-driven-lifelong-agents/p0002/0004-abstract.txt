historical lessons and acquired skills into effective guidance for novel situations.

experience base that stores knowledge accumulated from past interactions [ 11, 3, 10, 12, 4]. By querying this
repository at decision time, agents can in principle recall relevant facts, reuse successful strategies, and avoid repeating
