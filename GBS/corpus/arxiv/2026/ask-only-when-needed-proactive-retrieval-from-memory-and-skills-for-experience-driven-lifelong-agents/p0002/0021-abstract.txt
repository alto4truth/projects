The similarity term ensures relevance to the current query, while the priority term softly favors entries with demonstrated
utility. This typed decomposition enables a single query to return complementary evidence (what is true, what happened
before) and guidance (what to do, what to avoid), mitigating the redundancy inherent in monolithic memory systems.
