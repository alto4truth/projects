Online evolution of interactive agents.A parallel line of research enables agents to improve autonomously through
interaction. Within-episode methods such as iterative self-critique [9] strengthen single-episode capabilities but do not
accumulate cross-episode experience. Cross-episode approaches pursue continual improvement through two largely
independent channels. Memory-centric methods accumulate textual experience: Reflexion [10] stores verbal reflections
from past failures, V oyager [3] builds reusable skill libraries, and ExpeL [ 12] distills lessons from trial outcomes.
