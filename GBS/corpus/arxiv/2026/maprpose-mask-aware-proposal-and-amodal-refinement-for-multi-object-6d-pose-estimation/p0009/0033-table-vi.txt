V. CONCLUSION
We present MAPRPose, a hierarchical 6D pose estimation
framework designed to harness visible masks as explicit
geometric priors, mitigating the impact of severe occlusion and background clutter in zero-shot scenarios. The
MAPP module systematically prunes the hypothesis space
via mask-constrained correspondences. Simultaneously, the
AMPR mechanism rectifies truncation effects through joint
amodal prediction and dynamic ROI normalization, bolstering
robustness against partial visibility. By transcending conventional CAD-based rendering in favor of a tensorized RGBXYZ re-projection scheme, our pipeline facilitates massive
parallelization across multiple instances and hypotheses. This
architecture achieves a 43× increase in inference throughput
over FoundationPose while maintaining competitive precision.
