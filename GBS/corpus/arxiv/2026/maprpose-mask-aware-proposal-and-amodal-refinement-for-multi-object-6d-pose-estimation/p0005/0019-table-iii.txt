D. Ablation Studies
While our method achieves a balance between precision and
efficiency across multiple benchmarks, this section dissects the
specific contributions of our key components. We evaluate the
mask-aware proposal module on LINEMOD and the amodal
mask prediction module on the LM-O and YCB-V datasets to
analyze their robustness against occlusion and clutter.
(1) Analysis of the MAPP Module:To evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed MAPP module, we compare a hybrid
variant,MAPP+FP(where MAPP generates coarse proposals
for FoundationPose [24]), with the original FoundationPose
pipeline. As shown in Table III, MAPP+FP achieves an
ADD-0.1d accuracy of 99.4%, only 0.5% lower than the
full FoundationPose (99.9%) that relies on 252 hypotheses.
This marginal drop indicates that the constrained search space
preserves the high-quality hypotheses required for downstream
refinement, while significantly improving sampling efficiency.
