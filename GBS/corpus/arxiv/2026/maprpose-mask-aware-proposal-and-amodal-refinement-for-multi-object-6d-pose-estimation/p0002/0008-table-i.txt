For the BOP datasets, we adopt the Average Recall (AR)
as the unified metric, which is the arithmetic mean of three
symmetry-aware error functions: VSD (Visible Surface Discrepancy), MSSD (Maximum Symmetry-Aware Surface Distance),
and MSPD (Maximum Symmetry-Aware Projection Distance).
The final score is calculated as:
AR= 1
3 (ARVSD +AR MSSD +AR MSPD).(22)
Each component ARM is obtained by averaging the recall over
a set of standard error thresholds. This multi-metric approach
ensures a comprehensive evaluation of 3D geometry alignment
and 2D projection accuracy.
