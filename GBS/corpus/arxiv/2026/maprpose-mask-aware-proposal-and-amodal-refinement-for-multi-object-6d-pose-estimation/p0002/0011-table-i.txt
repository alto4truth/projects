(1) Performance on the LINEMOD Dataset:We first evaluate
our method on the LINEMOD dataset and report the quantitative results in Table I. As shown, our approach achieves stateof-the-art performance across all object categories. Specifically,
it achieves 100.0% accuracy on several categories, such as
benchvise,cam, anddrill, while consistently maintaining above
98% on the remaining ones. Compared with representative
baselines including LatentFusion [58], OnePose [60], GS-Pose
[63], and Geo6D [64], our method demonstrates superior
accuracy. We attribute these gains to the effective use of
geometric constraints and mask-aware representations, which
enables robust pose estimation in minimally textured tabletop
scenarios.
