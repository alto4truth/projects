B. Experimental Setup
We conducted three sets of experiments to evaluate the
accuracy and efficiency of our method across various scenarios.
On the LINEMOD dataset, we utilize ground-truth masks to
assess the performance of our refinement module under the
ADD-0.1d metric, reporting both accuracy and single-object
runtime latency on an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090 GPU. For
the more challenging BOP Challenge benchmarks, including
LM-O, YCB-V , and T-LESS, evaluations are performed on
an NVIDIA A100 GPU using object masks pre-estimated by
SAM-6D [38]. To maintain a consistent computational flow as
described in Section III, we adopt a tensorized batching strategy
for all refinement experiments. By setting N = 5 and B = 7, our
framework processes N × B = 35 hypothesis-reference pairs
in a single unified forward pass. This parallel configuration,
executed over i = 4 iterations, allows MAPRPose to achieve
over 90% GPU utilization on an NVIDIA A100 GPU. In
contrast, FoundationPose processes only one hypothesis at a
time and under-utilizes the GPU.
