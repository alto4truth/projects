Our evaluation protocols focus on both accuracy and system
throughput. For the BOP benchmark, we evaluate the Average
Recall (AR) and record the total processing time per image.
This metric accounts for the simultaneous handling of multiple
object instances within a single scene, providing a realistic
measure of multi-object inference speed. Additionally, we conducted ablation studies to systematically examine the influence
of key components on both accuracy and speed, including the
number of refinement iterations, the top- K (K= 7 ) parameter
for hypothesis selection, and the contribution of the amodal
mask prediction module.
C. Pose Estimation Results and Comparison
We evaluate the proposed MAPRPose on the LINEMOD
and BOP benchmarks to demonstrate its effectiveness and
robustness in 6D pose estimation for novel objects. In all
experiments, following the standard protocols, we report the
Average Recall (AR) and the ADD(-S) metrics to provide a
comprehensive comparison against state-of-the-art baselines.
