Finetuning Methods.Although we integrate our MOSS module into LST
framework [93] for efficient action recognition in previous experiments, MOSS is
also compatible with various finetuning scenarios including full finetuning and
parameter-efficient finetuning (PEFT) [58,92]. Here we conduct experiments in
such scenarios. For full finetuning, we add temporal convolution blocks and a
single MOSS module to the CLIP-pretrained ViT-B [62] and train the entire
network following [87]. For PEFT, we integrate MOSS into AIM [92] and train
