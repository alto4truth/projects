We evaluate our framework both quantitatively and qualitatively to assess scalability, reliability, and
semantic consistency using the following metrics:
Human Agreement (%)- percentage of evaluators agreeing with generated questions or answers.
Accuracy (%)- correctness of binary (Yes/No) and multiple-choice responses.
BLEU- 𝑛-gram precision measuring lexical overlap between model-generated answers and the
ground-truth answers in the dataset.
BERTScore (F1)- semantic similarity between model-generated and human-written captions.
CIDEr- consensus-based metric that measures similarity between generated captions and multiple
human references using TF-IDF weighted𝑛-grams.
SPICE- semantic propositional metric that evaluates agreement between generated and reference
captions at the level of objects, attributes, and relations.
