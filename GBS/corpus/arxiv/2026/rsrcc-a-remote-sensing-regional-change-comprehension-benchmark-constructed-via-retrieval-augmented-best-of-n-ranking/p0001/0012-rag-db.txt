4 Results
Dataset.TheLEVIR-CDdataset consists of high-resolution satellite image pairs (512×512 px,
≈0.5m/pixel ) capturing diverse urban and suburban scenes. Although LEVIR-CD follows a fixed
resolution, our framework is resolution-agnostic: all filtering, similarity, and region-based operations
are normalized by image dimensions, enabling deployment across datasets with varying spatial scales.
