H Robustness to Random Crops
To further validate the effectiveness of the proposed filtering and retrieval pipeline, we conduct an
additional robustness experiment based on randomly sampled image regions. Specifically, we extract
random spatial crops from the input images, without conditioning on any detected change regions or
semantic cues, and process them through the full pipeline.
