Question: What is the primary change observed between the two images?
A. A new residential building has been constructed.
B. A large area of vegetation has been cleared for a new park.
C. An existing building has undergone significant renovation.
D. A new road has been paved through the area.
The correct answer: A.
Question: What change is visible between the two images?
A. An existing building has been expanded.
B. A new road has been built.
C. A vacant lot has been developed with a new building.
D. Several trees have been planted.
The correct answer: C.
