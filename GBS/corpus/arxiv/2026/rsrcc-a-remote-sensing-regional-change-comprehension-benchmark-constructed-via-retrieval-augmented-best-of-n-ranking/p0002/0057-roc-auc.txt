I Class Distribution Analysis
Table 5 and Figure 14 present the distribution of detected changes across semantic categories in
our generated dataset (test only). The results indicate a clear dominance of thebuildingandtree
classes, which together account for over 64% of all detected changes. This imbalance aligns with the
urban–suburban nature of the underlying LEVIR-CD imagery, where most structural and vegetation
modifications occur.
