Table 6 provides a quantitative summary of our dataset after the automated filtering and validation
stages. Out of the total 10,007 processed image pairs, 89,031 contain at least one verified change,
while 37,100 are classified asno-changesamples. In total, the dataset includes 126,131 individual
annotated changes, demonstrating both large-scale coverage and a significant volume of descriptive
data. These statistics confirm the robustness of our automated pipeline in producing reliable, diverse
examples suitable for high-capacity change detection and captioning tasks.
