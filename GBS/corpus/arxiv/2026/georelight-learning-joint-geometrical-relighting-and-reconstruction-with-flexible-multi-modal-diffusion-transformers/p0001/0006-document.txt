In summary, our contributions are:
• A unified Multi-Modal Diffusion Transformer framework
that jointly generates relit images, intrinsic color, surface
normal details, and 3D geometry.
• iNOD, a novel and V AE-friendly depth representation
that isotropically preserves 3D geometry in latent space.
• A strategic mixed-data training methodology that combines synthetic data with auto-labeled real-world images
to achieve both geometric accuracy and photorealism.
