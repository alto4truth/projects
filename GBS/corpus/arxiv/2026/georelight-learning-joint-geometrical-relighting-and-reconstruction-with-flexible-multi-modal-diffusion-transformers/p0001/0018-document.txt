4.3. Comparison with State-of-the-art
Relighting PerformanceWe first evaluate our primary task
of relighting. Tab. 3 shows the quantitative comparison on
our synthetic, light stage, and HumanOLAT datasets. Our
method significantly outperforms all open-source generalpurpose baselines, including DiffusionRenderer (DR), NeuralGaffer, and DiLightNet, across all datasets and metrics.
We observe that the gap on HumanOLAT is smaller than our
hold-out evaluation data, which can be possible due to the
artifacts in the ground-truth. Due to data license limitation,
we could not obtain quantitative results for closed-source
