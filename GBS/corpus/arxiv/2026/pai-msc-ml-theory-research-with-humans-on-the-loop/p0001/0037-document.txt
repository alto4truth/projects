Campaign orchestration.The optional campaign layer uses heartbeat scripts, cron, or OpenClaw
to repeatedly launch or resume runs, validate stage artifacts, distill stage memory, track budget, attempt
autonomous repair, and notify the user. This extends the artifact-centric philosophy beyond a single run.
4.4 Operational model
pAI/MScis designed as a human-on-the-loop system. Asteeris a deliberate human intervention—distinct
from the graph’s own automatic loopbacks—that changes the direction, constraints, or scope of a run. The
current release exposes steering through: the initial task specification; a TCP control socket and HTTP API
during execution; resume from a named stage; injection of context files through aninputs/directory; and
campaign-level control for multi-stage pipelines.
