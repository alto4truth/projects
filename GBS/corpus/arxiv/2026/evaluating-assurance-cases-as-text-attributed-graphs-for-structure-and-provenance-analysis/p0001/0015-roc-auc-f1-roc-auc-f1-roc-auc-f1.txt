9.2 Internal Validity
The dataset used in this study, although carefully verified and curated to ensure the reliability and validity of human-authored samples, is relatively small. This limitation may affect the statistical
power of our analyses and introduce potential biases that could
impact the observed model performance. The restricted dataset
size may also limit the ability of the models to generalise effectively across diverse assurance case structures.However, publicly
available human-authored assurance cases are scarce in practice in our setting, which reflects the situation whether in
academia or industry due to data sensitivity.
Our uncertainty analysis does not include variation over the LLM
parameters. While this limits conclusions about full model variance,
our evaluation reflects realistic deployment settings where LLM
inference is performed via fixed APIs and variability from stochastic
generation and prompt variation.
