9 Threats to Validity
9.1 Construct Validity
Using link prediction and classification as proxies for argument
quality may capture structural patterns without ensuring logical
soundness. Additionally, the GNN might detect artefacts from the
synthetic edge-generation prompt rather than intrinsic LLM reasoning styles. Finally, our findings are currently tied to GPT-4o-specific
structural signatures and the limited faithfulness of existing GNN
explanation tools.
