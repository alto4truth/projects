3 Near-Future Policy Optimization
NPO follows a simple idea: for a prompt that the current policy struggles with, the most useful auxiliary
trajectory may come from a near-future checkpoint of the same training run. Rather than designing intricate
rewards, NPO modifies only the source of one trajectory inside each rollout group.
