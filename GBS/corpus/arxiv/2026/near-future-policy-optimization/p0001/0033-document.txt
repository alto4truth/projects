Figure 4Training dynamics of (Auto)NPO compared with vanilla GRPO.Red shaded regionsin panels (a)
and (b) highlight AutoNPO’s intervention windows.(a)Training reward dynamics: AutoNPO stays above GRPO
throughout, with the gap widening step-wise after each intervention window.(b)Generation entropy: GRPO undergoes
a steady entropy collapse, while AutoNPO’s interventions actively re-expand exploration and preserve a substantially
higher-entropy policy.(c)IS-correction ablation on the late-stage intervention: the two NPO variants with and without
exact importance-sampling correction both clearly outperform GRPO, indicating that IS correction is marginal for
NPO. Thick lines are smoothed curves; thin lines show raw logged values.
