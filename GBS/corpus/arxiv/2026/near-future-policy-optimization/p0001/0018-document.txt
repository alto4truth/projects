3.2 Two Manual Interventions
We first validate NPO through two manual interventions at different training stages, treating them as probes
ofwhennear-future guidance helps. Both share the mechanism described above and differ only in howπ(t+∆)
is obtained and which training window is replayed.
Early-Stage Bootstrapping.In the first few training steps, most rollout groups contain no correct trajectory
and the gradient signal collapses. The early intervention targets this cold-start regime: we train the base
model for a short scout segment and take its final checkpoint asπ(t+∆). We then restart training from the
same base weights and use the scout checkpoint to supply guidance trajectories during the initial window.
