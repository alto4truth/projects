For evaluation, we combined cross-group Swiss-system pairing with zero-shot LLM-based pairwise com-parison. In each round, generated ideas were paired with ideas from real papers based on current scores, with random pairing in the first round and score-based matching thereafter. Each pair was compared by an LLM along four dimensions—novelty, sci-entific rigor, clarity, and impact—and the better idea received 1 point. Scores were up-dated after each round, and the process was repeated for five rounds while avoiding rematches.
