Quality Score: Utilizing the Swiss-system tournament and zero-shot LLM prompt-ing method to evaluate the quality of research ideas, specifically, the ranking is deter-mined through pairwise comparisons to judge which idea is superior. Each idea under-goes 5 rounds of comparison, with 1 point awarded for each winning comparison. This quality assessment method has been proven superior to direct comparison or scoring (Si et al., 2024). Ideas with scores greater than or equal to 5 are considered high-scoring ideas.
