In this study, combinatorial innovation serves as the primary theoretical foundation. Our framework operationalizes this principle by enabling LLM agents to retrieve, rein-terpret, and recombine knowledge from existing literature. The iterative planning mech-anism reflects structured reasoning ideas related to TRIZ, while the multi-agent design encourages exploration beyond dominant research trajectories.
