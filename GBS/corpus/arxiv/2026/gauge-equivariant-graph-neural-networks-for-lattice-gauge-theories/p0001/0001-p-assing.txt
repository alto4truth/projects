of a gauge theory is local redundancy: physically equivalent configurations are related by independent symmetry transformations at each lattice site. On the lattice,
this structure is realized by assigning gauge degrees of
freedom to links, rather than to sites or continuous connections. Concretely, group-valued variablesU ij ∈ Gare
