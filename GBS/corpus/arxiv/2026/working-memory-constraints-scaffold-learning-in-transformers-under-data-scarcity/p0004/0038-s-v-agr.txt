B Structural Probing Analysis
Our structural probing analysis applies the derivational probing framework of Someya et al. (2025),
which extends the structural probe of Hewitt and
Manning (2019) by making explicit the layer-wise
derivation of syntactic structures in neural language
models. The method trains a diagnostic linear projection B∈R k×d over a model’s contextual embeddings hi ∈R d, such that the squared L2 distance between projected embeddings approximates
the tree distance between the corresponding tokens
in the syntactic dependency parse:
