E Prompting Templates
We present the detailed templates used for data
construction and model training. First, to ensure
standard dialogue state tracking, we adhere to a predefined schema of intents and slots. As illustrated
in Figure 6, representative intents include requesting product details (i.e., REQUEST:GET), comparing
items (i.e., REQUEST:COMPARE), and adding items
to the shopping cart (i.e., REQUEST:ADD_TO_CART).
Corresponding slots may include attributes such as
customer review, color, and price.
