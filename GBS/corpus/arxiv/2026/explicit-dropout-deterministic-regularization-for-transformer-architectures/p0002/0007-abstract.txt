2. Related Works

method to improve generalization and reduce overfitting by
preventing co-adaptation. It has since become a standard
component of deep neural networks, including convolutional, feed-forward, and Transformer architectures. From
a theoretical perspective, dropout has been interpreted as
training an implicit ensemble of subnetworks [29] or as approximateBayesianinferenceundervariationalassumptions
[8].
