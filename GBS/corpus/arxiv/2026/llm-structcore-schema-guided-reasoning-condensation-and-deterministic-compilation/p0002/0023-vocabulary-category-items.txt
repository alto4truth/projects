6 Related Work
Clinical information extraction has a long history, from rule-based systems
to modern LLM approaches. The i2b2/VA challenges [13] and ShARe/CLEF
eHealth tasks [12] established key clinical NLP benchmarks for structured
information extraction from unstructured clinical text. More recently, large
language models have demonstrated the potential to encode substantial
clinical knowledge [10], with domain-specific models like Med-Gemini [9]
achieving strong results on medical question answering and clinical reasoning
tasks.
