5.5 Limitations of the 9-Key Condensation
The choice of exactly 9 domain keys represents a trade-off. Fewer keys
(e.g., 3–4) would further simplify Stage 1 but lose domain-level organization,

recall but approach the complexity of direct 134-item extraction, negating
