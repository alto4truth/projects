This dynamic sampling dramatically improves RL efficiency by filtering out

4 Experiments
To evaluate the effectiveness of V-tableR1, we construct a comprehensive training and evaluation pipeline across multiple tabular reasoning benchmarks. Our
evaluation encompasses two primary tasks: Table Fact Verification (TFV) and
Table Question Answering (TQA).
