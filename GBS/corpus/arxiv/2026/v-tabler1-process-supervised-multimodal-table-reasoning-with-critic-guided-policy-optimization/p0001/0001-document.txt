Abstract.We introduce V-tableR1, a process-supervised reinforcement
learning framework that elicits rigorous, verifiable reasoning from multimodal large language models (MLLMs). Current MLLMs trained solely
on final outcomes often treat visual reasoning as a black box, relying on
superficial pattern matching rather than performing rigorous multi-step
inference. While Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards could
enforce transparent reasoning trajectories, extending it to visual domains
remains severely hindered by the ambiguity of grounding abstract logic
