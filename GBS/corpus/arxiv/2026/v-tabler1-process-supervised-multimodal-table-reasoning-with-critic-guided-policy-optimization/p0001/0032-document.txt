4.3 Ablation Studies
To systematically dissect the source of V-tableR1’s performance gains, we conduct ablation studies focusing on the core components of our proposed optimization algorithm. We evaluate the models on both the TFV and TQA benchmarks,
using the Qwen3-VL-2B architecture as our base model for these experiments.
