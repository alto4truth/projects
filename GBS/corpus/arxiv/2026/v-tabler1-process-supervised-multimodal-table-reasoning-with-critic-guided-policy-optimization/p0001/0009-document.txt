–Process-Guided Direct Alignment Policy Optimization (PGPO). We propose PGPO, a novel RL algorithm tailored for long-horizon visual reasoning. Building upon the stability of Direct Alignment Policy Optimization
(DAPO) and the dynamic sampling efficiency of Length-aware Sampling for
Policy Optimization (LSPO), PGPO leverages fine-grained process feedback
to optimize the policy model. This unified objective fundamentally shifts the
learning paradigm from guessing correct outcomes to mastering verifiable,
robust reasoning trajectories.
