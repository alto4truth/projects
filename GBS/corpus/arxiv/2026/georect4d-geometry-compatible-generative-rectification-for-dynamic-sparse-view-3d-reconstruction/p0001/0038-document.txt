5 Discussion
Conclusion.This paper presents GeoRect4D designed to tackle the highly illposed problem of dynamic 3D scene reconstruction from extremely sparse multiview videos. By synergistically coupling a robust explicit dynamic 3D Gaussian
Splatting substrate with a degradation-aware generative prior, our approach effectively overcomes prevalent issues such as geometric collapse, trajectory drift,
and floating artifacts. Extensive evaluations demonstrate that GeoRect4D establishes a new state-of-the-art in both rendering fidelity and perceptual realism.
By successfully hallucinating fine-grained details while strictly preserving spatiotemporal consistency, this work bridges the gap between physical geometric
constraints and 2D generative priors for high-quality free-viewpoint video synthesis.
