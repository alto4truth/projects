Evaluation Metrics.We report the PSNR and SSIM [60] of all methods
to evaluate their reconstruction quality and modeling capability, alongside the
average LPIPS [74] for perceptual quality. For the temporal consistency evaluation of the reconstructed sequences, we adopt tLPIPS [9] and tOF [9], and use
RAFT [55] to compute optical flow. We also report no-reference metrics such as
MUSIQ [25] and MANIQA [68] as references to evaluate the visual quality of
the enhanced images.
