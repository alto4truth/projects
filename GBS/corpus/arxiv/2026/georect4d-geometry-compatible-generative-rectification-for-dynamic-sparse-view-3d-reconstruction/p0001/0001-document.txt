Abstract.Reconstructing dynamic 3D scenes from sparse multi-view
videos is highly ill-posed, often leading to geometric collapse, trajectory
drift, and floating artifacts. Recent attempts introduce generative priors
to hallucinate missing content, yet naive integration frequently causes
structural drift and temporal inconsistency due to the mismatch between stochastic 2D generation and deterministic 3D geometry. In this
paper, we propose GeoRect4D, a novel unified framework for sparseview dynamic reconstruction that couples explicit 3D consistency with
