B.2 Scoring Assessment Prompts
To enable a scalable, multi-dimensional, and nuanced evaluation of
the generated music notations, ONOTE employs anLLM-as-a-Judge
paradigm. While traditional string-matching metrics fall short in
assessing the musicality and structural validity of complex scores,
state-of-the-art LLMs can effectively serve as automated expert
evaluators when guided by rigorous rubrics.
