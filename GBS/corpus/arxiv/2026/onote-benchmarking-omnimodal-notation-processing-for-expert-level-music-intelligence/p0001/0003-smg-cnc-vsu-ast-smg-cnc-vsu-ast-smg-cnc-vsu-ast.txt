5.3 Dimensional Analysis in Audio-to-Symbolic
Transcription
The breakdown of AST metrics reveals differences in acoustictemporal alignment capabilities, which vary depending on the
spatial dimensionality of the target notation. Specifically, models
generally exhibit higher accuracy when mapping audio to linear,
one-dimensional formats, compared to navigating the complex,
two-dimensional coordinate space required for Standard Staff.
