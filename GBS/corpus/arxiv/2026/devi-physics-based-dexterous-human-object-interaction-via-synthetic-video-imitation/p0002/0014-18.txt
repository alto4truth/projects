A.4 Generating HOI Video
To generate an HOI video from the rendered images, we use a pre-trained video diffusion model,
Wan [49] with additional LightX2V [22] LoRA for faster inference. Generating a single video takes
about 10 minutes on an NVIDIA A6000 GPU. For the text prompt, we use following format:
A person [action] [object] with [hand index] [detail]. Both hands are
visible in the frame. Stationary camera.
