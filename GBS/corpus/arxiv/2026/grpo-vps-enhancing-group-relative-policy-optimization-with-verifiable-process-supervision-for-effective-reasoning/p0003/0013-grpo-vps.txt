3.1 REASONINGPROCESSSEGMENTATION
Recent studies have revealed that performance gains in RLVR are primarily driven by critical decision points
characterized by high token-level uncertainty (Yang et al., 2025; Wang et al., 2025). Inspired by SPO (Guo
et al., 2025), we adopt an Adaptive Entropy-based Cutpoint Partition strategy, leveraging token-level entropy
to robustly identify reasoning ”junctions” where the model’s trajectory is likely to diverge.
