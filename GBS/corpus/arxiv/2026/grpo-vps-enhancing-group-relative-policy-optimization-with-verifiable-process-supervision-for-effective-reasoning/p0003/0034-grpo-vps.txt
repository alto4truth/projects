5 CONCLUSION
We present GRPO-VPS, a model-free and verifier-free method that augments GRPO with segment-level
credit assignment derived from conditional answer probabilities. Our method generates dense and interpretable supervision signals aligned with the model’s internal decision flow, enabling more efficient and
targeted policy optimization. Experiments on four math reasoning benchmarks show that our method consistently improves both accuracy and reasoning conciseness over strong RL baselines and segment-aware
