Comparison with outcome supervised RL.Compared to GRPO and its recent variants, GRPOVPS achieves the highest overall accuracy across all benchmarks while substantially shortening the reasoning length. On Qwen2.5-Math-1.5B, it improves Pass@1 by more than 3% over GRPO and sustains
a comparable margin on the 7B model, with an average reduction of 10–11% in output length. GRPOVPS achieves larger gains in accuracy and produces shorter outputs. These results suggest that our method
improves both effectiveness and efficiency of policy updates, leading to more focused reasoning traces. On
Gemma-2-2B-it, our method further reduces the output length compared to GRPO while achieving higher
accuracy. Although the Gemma base model exhibits shorter responses, training with reinforcement learning
unlocks its long-chain reasoning behavior, as further evidenced in Appendix A.4.1.
Comparison with model-based process supervision.Compared to S-GRPO, which uses multi-rollout
early-exit probing to assign segment-level rewards, our method achieves higher accuracy and shorter outputs.
