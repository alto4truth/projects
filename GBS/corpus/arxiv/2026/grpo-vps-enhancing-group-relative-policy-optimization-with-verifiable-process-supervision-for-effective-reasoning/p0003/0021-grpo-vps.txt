Evaluation setup.We evaluate on four widely used math reasoning benchmarks, including the test sets of
MATH, AIME 2024 (MAA, 2024), AMC23 (MAA, 2023) and OlympiadBench (He et al., 2024a). We set
temperature to 1.0, toppset to 1, and maximum output length set to 3,072 tokens for inference. Due to the
high variance of the outputs from reasoning models, we report the average Pass@1 over 4 runs. To ensure
accurate evaluation, we utilize Math-Verify1 to check for answer equivalence.
