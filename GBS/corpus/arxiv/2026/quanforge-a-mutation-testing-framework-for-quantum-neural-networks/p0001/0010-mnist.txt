For OOD and Adv, half of the original inputs are replaced with abnormal ones. These suites contain

high-confidence inputs far from the decision boundary, (2)Skewed, class-imbalanced inputs with
ratios of 10:1 for binary tasks and 10:1:1 for ternary tasks, and (3)Small, a half-size suite. Except for
Small, all suites have the same size as Ori.
