We observe that the Add operator is relatively mild in mutation strength. It generally exhibits
a lower KR and a higher NR. This finding is consistent with a previous study [ 51]. Currently,
QuanForge adopts a single MO per mutant. Based on the performance of MOs, future work can
explore combinations of different MOs to produce mutants with greater complexity and diversity.
Moreover, a prioritization strategy for MOs could be developed to improve the generation efficiency
of effective mutants, e.g., reducing the weight of MOs that tend to produce trivial mutants.
