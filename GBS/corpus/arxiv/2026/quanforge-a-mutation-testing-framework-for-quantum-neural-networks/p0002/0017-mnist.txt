4.5 RQ2: Sensitivity of Different Regions in QNNs to Mutation
In addition to measuring test quality, mutation testing can also probe internal model behaviors
by targeting specific regions of the network [48]. Inspired by this, here we investigate whether
QuanForge can serve a similar role in analyzing quantum circuits and assessing structural robustness.
Specifically, we vary the scope, depth, and qubit ranges of the mutation to systematically scan
different parts of the circuit. This enables a fine-grained analysis of how mutations at various
depths or on different qubits affect QNN performance. We report the mean accuracy of all effective
