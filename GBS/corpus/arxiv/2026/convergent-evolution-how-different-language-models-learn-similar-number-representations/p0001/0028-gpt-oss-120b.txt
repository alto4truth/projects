4.2 Structural Attribution to Architecture and Optimizer
We now fix the pretraining data, and vary the architecture and optimizer. We train a 300M
parameter Transformer model and two linear RNNs (Gated DeltaNet and Mamba-2 (Dao &
Gu, 2024)) whose architectural and training details are shown in § B.1. We vary two different
optimizers for training LLMs: AdamW and Muon. Figure 5 shows the results.
