3.3 Phase 2: Multi-faceted Quality
Assessment
The second phase conducts a multi-faceted evaluation of each decomposed response along three
orthogonal dimensions: logical coherence, factual
accuracy, and image-text consistency. We employ Qwen2.5-VL-7B-Instruct-AWQ (Bai et al.,
2025) as an automated auditor, which assigns interpretable 1–5 scores and textual rationales based on
a detailed rubric. This step provides fine-grained
diagnostics of different error types while producing
standardized quality scores that can be aggregated
for ranking and selection.
