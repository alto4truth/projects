2 Related Work
Vision-language data curation has progressed from
coarse pre-training filters to instruction-tuning
strategies, yet scalable and fine-grained evaluation
remains largely missing. As a result, most existing methods still rely on shallow quality proxies,
limiting their ability to diagnose subtle semantic,
logical, or factual defects.
