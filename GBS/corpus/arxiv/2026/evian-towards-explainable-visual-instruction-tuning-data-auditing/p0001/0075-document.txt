Experimental Setup.We established a rigorous baseline using the “Origin Full” configuration,
where the Qwen2-VL-2B model was fine-tuned on
the complete pool of 300,000 raw samples derived
from the eight foundational datasets prior to any
defect injection. This represents the upper bound
of data quantity without our intervention. This
baseline was compared against the “EVIAN (10K)”
model, which was fine-tuned on the top-10,000
samples selected by our framework. Both models
utilized identical model architectures and training
hyperparameters and were evaluated using the standard VLMEvalKit suite to ensure a fair comparison
focused strictly on data efficiency and quality.
