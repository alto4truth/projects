8 Limitations
While EVIAN demonstrates strong effectiveness
for fine-grained auditing of visual instruction data,
several limitations remain. First, the framework
relies on large pre-trained multimodal language
models for both response decomposition and quality evaluation. Despite strong alignment between
automated scores and human judgments, the auditing process may still inherit biases, blind spots, or
reasoning tendencies from the underlying models,
particularly in ambiguous or culturally sensitive
scenarios.
