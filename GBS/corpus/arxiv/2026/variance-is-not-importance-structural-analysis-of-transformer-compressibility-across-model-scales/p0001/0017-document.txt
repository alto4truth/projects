5 Extended Analysis
5.1 Spectral Compression via DCT
We applied the Discrete Cosine Transform (DCT) to weight matrices, hypothesizing that neural
network weights—like images—might have concentrated frequency spectra amenable to JPEGlike compression.
Definition 1(Spectral Energy Concentration).The Gini coefficient of the squared DCT coefficients measures how concentrated the spectral energy is. A Gini score near 1.0 indicates high
concentration (compressible); near 0.0 indicates uniform spread (incompressible).
