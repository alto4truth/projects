3 Methodology
3.1 Models and Data
We experiment on two models:
•GPT-2(124M parameters, 12 transformer blocks, hidden dimension 768)
•Mistral 7B(7.24B parameters, 32 transformer blocks, hidden dimension 4096)
Calibration and evaluation data are drawn from WikiText-2 [14]. We use a strict train/evaluation
split: calibration data for fitting (32 samples×256 tokens = 8,192 tokens) and held-out data
for perplexity evaluation (16 samples).
