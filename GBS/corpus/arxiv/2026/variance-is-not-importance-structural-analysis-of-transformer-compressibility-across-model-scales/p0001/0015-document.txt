4.5 Finding 5: 30% of Tokens Are Easy
Finding 5.Approximately 30% of tokens can be classified as “computationally easy,” independently confirmed through two methods on two models.
Method 1: Trained exit heads.On Mistral 7B, a dedicated prediction head trained at
block 15 (500 steps, RMSNorm + Linear) achieves 30.2% top-1 agreement with the full model.
Extended training plateaus at∼30%.
Method 2: KL sensitivity.On GPT-2, 30.1% of tokens show low KL divergence (below
median) when late blocks (8–11) are destroyed via INT2 quantization.
