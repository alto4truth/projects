2. Related Works
Noisy label learning in centralized setting.In centralized
learning, where all training data are aggregated and processed on a central server, multiple methods have been proposed to address the noisy label challenge. One prominent
category is sample selection and reweighting, which aims
to identify clean samples during training. In [5], two neural networks are trained simultaneously, and each network
selects small-loss samples to update its peer. To mitigate
the risk that both networks converge to the same erroneous
predictions, [23] further introduces a disagreement-update
mechanism before the cross-update.
