K. Human Baseline Details
To calibrate the difficulty of OMIBench against human performance, we conduct a small-scale human study
on a uniformly sampled 52-problem subset spanning biology, chemistry, mathematics, and physics. We
recruit four participants: two domain experts (PhD/Master’s students in STEM-related fields) and two
trained non-experts (undergraduates without direct domain specialization). None of the participants has
seen OMIBench before the study.
