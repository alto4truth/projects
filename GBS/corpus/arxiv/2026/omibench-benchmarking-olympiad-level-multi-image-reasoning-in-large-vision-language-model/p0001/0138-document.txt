meaningful benchmark for measuring progress in multi-image Olympiad-level reasoning.
L. Single-Image Control Details
To distinguish the effect of cross-image reasoning from confounders such as visual volume, input length,
and OCR noise, we construct an information-equivalent single-image control. For each problem, all images
are concatenated in their original logical order into a single composite image, while the question text and
answer choices remain unchanged.
