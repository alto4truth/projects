For the correlation analysis between OMIBench and OlympiadBench, we evaluate three representative LVLMs:
GPT, Gemini, Qwen-VL, and InternVL. All models are used in their official API or checkpoint form without
any additional fine-tuning or task-specific adaptation. In order to reduce evaluation cresource consumption,
the partial results of OlympiadBench are taken from Wan et al.[55], Wang et al.[59], Team et al.[51], Yue
et al. [69], Shen et al. [48], Zhang et al. [73], Zha et al. [70], Team et al. [52], Yu et al. [67].
