Annotator Recruitment and Training.Annotators were selected from graduate students with prior
experience in machine learning or related quantitative fields. Before using the annotation platform, they
completed a written tutorial on task definitions, edge cases, and examples of acceptable and unacceptable
annotations; passed a 30-item calibration test spanning diverse problem types and difficulty levels with
at least 80% agreement with an expert gold standard; and signed a code-of-conduct and confidentiality
agreement on responsible data handling. We require all annotation experts to have at least one professional
competition experience and be trained to at least a bronze medal level.
