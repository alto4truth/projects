6.4. Can SFT or Tools Close the Gap?
Beyond prompting and test-time strategies, we ask whether (1) supervised fine-tuning (SFT) on existing
multi-image data, or (2) integration of external visual tools, can substantially narrow the gap on OMIBench.
Detailed numbers are reported in Appendix M (Tables 9 and 10).
