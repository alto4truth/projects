A.4.2. Disagreement Resolution and Escalation
Disagreements on critical fields, such as solution correctness or label assignment, triggered an automatic
escalation to a senior reviewer. These discrepancies were identified via logical inconsistencies (e.g., opposing
validity flags) or rating divergences of at least 2 points. A senior expert with domain experience then
examined the full annotation history to issue a binding decision: retaining the primary annotation, adopting
the auditor’s revision, or rewriting the content entirely with a supporting rationale.
