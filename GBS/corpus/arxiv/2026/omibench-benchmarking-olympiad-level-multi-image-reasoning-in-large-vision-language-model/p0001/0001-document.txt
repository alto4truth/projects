Abstract:
Large vision-language models (LVLMs) have made substantial advances in reasoning tasks at the Olympiad level.
Nevertheless, current Olympiad-level multimodal reasoning benchmarks for these models often emphasize
single-image analysis and fail to exploit contextual information across multiple images. We present OMIBench,
a benchmark designed to evaluate Olympiad-level reasoning when the required evidence is distributed over
multiple images. It contains problems from biology, chemistry, mathematics, and physics Olympiads, together
