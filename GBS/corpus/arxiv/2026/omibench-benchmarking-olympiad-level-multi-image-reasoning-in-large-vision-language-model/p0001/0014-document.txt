3.2. Data Annotation
The data annotation pipeline comprises four phases: data collection & selection, rationale annotation, quality
control, and classification labeling.

mathematics, and physics, with source distributions summarized in Table 3. The corpus integrates international Olympiads, national and regional contests, and mixed-complexity benchmarks, providing broad
Olympiad-level coverage across subfields in these disciplines.
