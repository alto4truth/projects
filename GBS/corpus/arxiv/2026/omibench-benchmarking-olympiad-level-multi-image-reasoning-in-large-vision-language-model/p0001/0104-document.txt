C.4. Performance Comparison between Accuracy and GPTScore

model ranking is preserved.Models with higher GPTScore also achieve higher accuracy under GPTScorebased evaluation, but accuracy systematically overestimates weaker models and underestimates stronger
ones, while largely preserving their relative ranking. To substantiate this, we manually analyze 100 instances
where accuracy and GPTScore disagree for Gemini-3-Pro-Preview and InternVL3.5-1B.
