Agreement with human ratings and cross-evaluator stability.We randomly sample200 answers generated by Gemini-3-Pro-Preview and obtain human ratings. We then compare these human scores with
GPTScore produced by three different evaluator models. As shown in Table 7, all Spearman correlation
