Remark5.2.The experiments above emphasize clustering quality rather than speed. In our
current reference implementation [11], weighted angle distance computations are substantially slower than the baselines, despite being linear-time per pair in principle: the bottleneck
is Python-level overhead combined with the need to computeO(N 2) pairwise distances for
DBSCAN, with no caching across parameter sweeps and no parallelism. We therefore report
timing only as a coarse reference (Table 4) and view performance optimization (vectorization, memoization of repeated trigonometric calls, and parallel distance-matrix construction)
as future work.
