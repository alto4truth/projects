Recent work by Petty et al. proposes a new edit-based distance designed to address some
of these weaknesses, with a particular focus on capturing repetition-robust similarity while
preserving an operational “editing” interpretation [15]. Their construction augments the
edit operations with a family of stutter-like transformations, but requires one to commit in
advance to a menu of permissible stutters and to perform substantial precomputation to
support them.
