•Methodology fixed before runs.The methodology described in Section 5.4 was

5.2.Datasets.We evaluate clustering quality on three labeled datasets of strings drawn
from public genomics repositories. Each dataset is normalized into a uniform three-column
schema
(label,sample id,sequence),
so that the clustering pipeline is agnostic to the data source and only depends on the string
distance.
