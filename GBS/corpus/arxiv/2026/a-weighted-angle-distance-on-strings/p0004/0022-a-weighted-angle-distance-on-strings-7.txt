Remark4.4.The algorithm above may be implemented purely with suffix arrays. One
builds the suffix array ofU=S#T$ and its LCP array, then constructs the LCP-interval
tree, which is isomorphic (as a compact trie of all suffixes) to the internal-node structure of
the suffix tree. The leaf-origin labels and the post-order aggregation of (occ S,occ T ) are then
carried out on this interval tree, and the same range-add formulas apply.
