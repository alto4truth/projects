5. Conclusion
In this work, we have introduced R-CoV , a visual chainof-verification method that triggers region-level processing
from LVLMs themselves to alleviate their own hallucinations. R-CoV mimics how humans comprehend intricate
visual information by delving into details in specific image
regions. Our method can be seamlessly applied to various
LVLMs without retraining or relying on external detection
