Our region-aware chain-of-verification (R-CoV) is a
training-free post-hoc correction method to alleviate object
hallucinations in LVLMs. The overall pipeline of R-CoV is
illustrated in Figure 2. It contains six stages: initial response
generation, entity extraction, coordinate generation, region
description, verification execution, and final response generation. We detail each stage as follows.
