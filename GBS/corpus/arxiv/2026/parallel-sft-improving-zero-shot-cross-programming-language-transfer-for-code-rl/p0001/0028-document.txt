7 Analysis
Inspired by the multilingual NLP literature (§2.3), we investigate whether the improved transferability correlates with
more PL-general representations. Specifically, we check if Parallel-SFT promotes the representation similarity between
standalone parallel programs (i.e., without contexts), as is standard in multilingual research (Wu et al., 2025b; Shen
et al., 2025; Gao et al., 2025;i.a.).
