Limitations
While Parallel-SFT demonstrates improvements in cross-PL transfer, our exploration was not exhaustive. We did not
perform a search over many design decisions, including SFT data formatting, curricula, or mixing ratios. Further
iterations on these could yield further gains, perhaps additionally leveraging typological similarities between PLs, which
would better inform transfer. Additionally, our experiments focused on fundamental coding tasks. We anticipate that the
benefits of Parallel-SFT extend to more complex settings, such as reasoning models and coding agents, but verifying
this remains an open question. We leave these explorations to future work.
