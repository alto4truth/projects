Implementation details.We implement our method with
PyTorch. In our experiments, we augment the bounding box
with a mask as the model input, expanding the bounding box
by a factor of 0.2 in all directions, resulting in an input area
1.44 times larger than the original. The iteration counts are set
to 40k, 20k, 7k, 75k and 75k for KINS, COCOA, COCOA-cls,
FISHBOWL and MOViD-A datasets, respectively. We utilize
the AdamW optimizer with a learning rate that decreases from
1e-4 to 5e-5 throughout training.
