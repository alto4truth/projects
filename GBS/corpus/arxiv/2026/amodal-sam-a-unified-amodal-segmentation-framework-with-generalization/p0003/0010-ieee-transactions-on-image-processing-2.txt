SAM-based adaptation.The remarkable generalization
capability of SAM has inspired numerous downstream adaptation [14]–[17] tailored for specialized domains. Notable
advancements include HQ-SAM [17], which introduces a
refined output token to capture intricate boundary details,
and MedSAM [16], which specializes the model for medical
imaging via large-scale domain-specific fine-tuning. Drawing inspiration from the adapter-based paradigm in natural
language processing [18], [19], recent frameworks such as
SAM-Adapter [14] and ViT-Adapter [20] utilize lightweight
bottleneck modules to inject domain-specific knowledge into
the frozen ViT backbone. These strategies preserve the robust
pre-trained representations while enabling efficient knowledge
transfer to downstream tasks with minimal computational
overhead.
