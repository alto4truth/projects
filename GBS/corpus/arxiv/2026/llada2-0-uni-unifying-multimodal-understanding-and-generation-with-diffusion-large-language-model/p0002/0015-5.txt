5.2.2 General Image Generation Performance
GenEval.Table 3 presents a comparison of model performance on the GenEval benchmark, which is
designed to evaluate object-centric T2I generation using compositional prompts with diverse object attributes.
LLaDA2.0-Uni demonstrates strong compositional capabilities, achieving an overall score of 0.89. This
performance is highly competitive, significantly outperforming all unified models and bridging the gap with
top-tier generation-only models. In particular, LLaDA2.0-Uni shows a clear advantage in spatial arrangement,
securing the highest Position score (0.90) across all evaluated models.
