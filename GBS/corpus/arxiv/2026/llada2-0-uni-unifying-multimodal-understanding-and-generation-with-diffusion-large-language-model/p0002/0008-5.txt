Baselines.To evaluate the multimodal performance of LLaDA2.0-Uni, we compare it against an extensive
set of baselines. We first compare LLaDA2.0-Uni with leading specialized VLMs, including Qwen2.5-VL7B (Qwen Team, 2025a) (AR-based) and LLaDA-V (You et al., 2025a) (diffusion-based). Furthermore, we
evaluate LLaDA2.0-Uni against state-of-the-art unified models categorized into: 1) AR-based models, such
as BAGEL (Deng et al., 2025) and InternVL-U (Tian et al., 2026); and 2) diffusion-based models, such as
Lumina-DiMOO (Xin et al., 2025a) and LLaDA-o (You et al., 2026).
