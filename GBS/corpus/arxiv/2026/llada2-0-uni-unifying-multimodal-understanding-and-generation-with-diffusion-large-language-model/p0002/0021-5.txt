UniGenBench.LLaDA2.0-Uni sets a new standard for unified models on UniGenBench (EN), achieving
a top overall score of 79.63 (Table 6). It performs consistently well across all ten dimensions, showing a
clear advantage in Logic (63.99) and Layout (90.30), where it even surpasses many specialized generation
models. These results demonstrate that LLaDA2.0-Uni effectively closes the performance gap between
general-purpose unified models and top-tier specialized models.
