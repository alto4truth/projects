Distributed Framework.We employ dFactory (InclusionAI, 2025) as the primary training engine for both
pre-training and post-training phases—a high-efficiency framework specifically optimized for Diffusion
Large Language Models. Built upon the VeOmni (Ma et al., 2025) distributed training ecosystem, dFactory
enables the flexible deployment of sophisticated parallelization strategies.
