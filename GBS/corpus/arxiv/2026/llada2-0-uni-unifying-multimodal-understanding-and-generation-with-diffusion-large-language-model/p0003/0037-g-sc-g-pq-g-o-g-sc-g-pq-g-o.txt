5.5.1 Analysis of SPRINT Acceleration
Table 13 evaluates SPRINT on nine multimodal benchmarks, covering both understanding (AI2D, OCRBench,
MathVista, ChartQA, DocVQA, MMMU, MMStar) and generation (GenEval, DPG). SPRINT shifts the average
score from 76.3 to 75.7 (−0.6) while accelerating generation from 24.3 to 39.8 TPS (1.6×). Several benchmarks
