Scene Initialization and Viewpoint Curation.A key aspect of our data generation is sampling diverse and meaningful camera viewpoints. For each indoor scene, we employ DBSCAN clustering [23] on the floor plan to partition
the space into distinct rooms. Within each room, we perform maximally dispersed viewpoint sampling. To ensure
these viewpoints are ”actionable,” we prioritize those containing more manipulable objects, guaranteeing each viewpoint can support a rich set of potential spatial operations.
