Qualitative results (Fig 3 and more in appendix) reveal
consistent trends: most models perform better on removal
than other operations, indicating deletion is easier than precise geometric manipulation. Emu3.5 produces the cleanest
removals with strongest spatial consistency. However, Ultra
and AnyEdit often fail to preserve object identity; AnyEdit,
BAGEL, and Omnigen2 introduce artifacts; AnyEdit frequently leaves targets unchanged; BAGEL sometimes misinterprets translation as camera motion. While BAGEL,
Emu3.5, and Qwen reliably follow referential cues, they
occasionally remove additional content, indicating finegrained localization remains challenging.
