The second category analyzes spatial SAR backscatter patterns to distinguish multiple deployment stages. Hoeser
et al. [2022] introduced this category by using three-month aggregated Sentinel-1 SAR data to differentiate between no
turbine, turbine foundation, and deployed turbine stages. However, the quarterly aggregation reduces temporal precision
and limits the ability to capture short-term events such as vessel interactions or precise phase transitions (e.g., from
open water to completed foundation, or from foundation to fully installed turbine).
