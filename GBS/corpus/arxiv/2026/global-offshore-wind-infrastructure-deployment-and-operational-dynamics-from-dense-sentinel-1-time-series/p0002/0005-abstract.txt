European Union aims to reach 300 GW of offshore wind energy installed capacity [European Commission, 2020]. China
plans to expand its combined installed solar and wind energy capacity to six times its 2020 level, reaching approximately
3,600 GW [United Nations, Department of Global Communications, 2025], with offshore wind representing a rapidly
growing component in this strategy. Considering these targets, the recent surge in offshore wind infrastructure
deployment is only the beginning of a much larger expansion over the coming decades with a regional focus in the
North Sea Basin and along the coast of China.
