Due to the near-polar orbit and right-looking acquisition geometry of Sentinel-1, images are acquired in ascending and
descending passes, meaning targets are observed either from west-to-east or east-to-west viewing directions. For vertical
metallic structures such as offshore wind turbines, this geometry produces characteristic radar signatures [Hoeser and
Kuenzer, 2022a].
