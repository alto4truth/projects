4 Method
4.1 Updated global offshore wind infrastructure detection
To derive offshore wind infrastructure locations at global scale, we updated the detection workflow proposed by Hoeser
et al. [2022]. The workflow consists of a global offshore wind farm proposal network, and a second-stage detector that
identifies individual infrastructure units within the proposed regions.
