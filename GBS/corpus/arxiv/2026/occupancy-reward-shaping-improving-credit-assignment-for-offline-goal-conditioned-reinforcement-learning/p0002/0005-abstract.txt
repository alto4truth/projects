2025) have shown a remarkable ability to capture the multi-modal distribution of future states an
agent may visit, i.e., the occupancy measure. This suggests that they must inherently possess the
ability to reason about long-term temporal effects of actions by encoding the geometry of the environment. Nonetheless, it remains unclear how to extract such temporal information from a learned
world model and leverage it for credit assignment.
