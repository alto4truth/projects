workflow: trajectory analysis identifies the latent
failure pattern, graph-based optimization formulates a structured corrective update, and the final

explicit reasoning protocol. The example also highlights a broader point: in complex agent systems,
effective optimization often requires procedural refinement rather than surface-level prompt rewriting.
By making these refinements modular and interpretable, TPGO supports both automated optimization and post-hoc inspection.
