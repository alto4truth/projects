2.2 Automatic Prompt Optimization
Recent efforts in automatic prompt optimization
have pursued three primary directions. Searchbased methods, such as PromptAgent (Wang et al.,
2023), leverage MCTS for strategic exploration.
Evolutionary algorithms like EvoPrompt (Tong
et al., 2025) iteratively evolve prompts to select for
high-performing candidates. In contrast, gradientbased approaches such as TextGrad (Yuksekgonul
et al., 2024) frame prompts as differentiable parameters, optimizing them through backpropagation.
A shared limitation of these methods is their
focus on textual content over system architecture.
