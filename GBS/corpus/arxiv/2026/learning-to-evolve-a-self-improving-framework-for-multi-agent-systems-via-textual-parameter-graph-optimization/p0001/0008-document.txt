2 Related Work
2.1 Language Agent Systems
While single-agent systems (SAS) such as the ReAct framework (Yao et al., 2023) offer simplicity
and efficiency, their capabilities are inherently limited. To overcome these limitations, LLM-based
multi-agent systems (MAS) have emerged as a powerful paradigm for complex problem-solving. The
prevailing design methodology involves decomposing a task into sub-tasks and assigning them to
specialized agents that emulate collaborative human teams (Park et al., 2023; Guo et al., 2024).
