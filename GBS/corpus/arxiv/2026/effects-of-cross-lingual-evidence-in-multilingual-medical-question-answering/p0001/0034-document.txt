6 Discussion
6.1 External Sources Analysis
Based on the results described in Section 5 and Appendices M-R, we can conclude that smaller <30B
parameter models benefit from the data provided
from external sources. Nevertheless, the obvious
gain is obtained for English and less frequently for
French, Spanish and Italian.
