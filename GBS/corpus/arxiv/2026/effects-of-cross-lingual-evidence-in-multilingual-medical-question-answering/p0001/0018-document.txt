3.3 Parametric knowledge of LLMs
Our initial results show that the performance of
the LLMs with more than 70B parameters excels
in medical QA in English without any additional
information, which leads us to hypothesize that
these LLMs pre-trained on an immense amount of
world knowledge encode sufficient information in
the medical domain within their parameters (AllenZhu and Li, 2023; Ju et al., 2024; He et al., 2025).

to generate answers to the queries used for web
search and generate an answer for the clinical case
question. We generate these explanations both in
