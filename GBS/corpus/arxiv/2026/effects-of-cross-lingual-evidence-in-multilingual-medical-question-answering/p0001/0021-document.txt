4.1 Language of Retrieval
We retrieve external evidence from the sources described in Section 3. Based on the multilingual
availability of the sources, we aim to investigate
an optimal strategy for multilingual retrieval in
MMQA tasks. Therefore, we obtain results in three
settings: monolingual, when retrieval is always
in one language, i.e., English; multilingual, when
the evidence is retrieved in the language of the
question; and cross-lingual, when one part of the
retrieval is done in English and the other in the
language of the question Liu et al. (2025). In our
experiments, we retrieve the same number of 10
documents for every setting.
