Limitations
This work has several limitations. Although our
conclusions are intended to be general, their applicability to other medical domains, as well as to
domains outside medicine, requires further investigation. Additionally, while we found that retrieving
10 documents yielded the highest impact, our experiments were limited to document counts of up to 32.
Due to computational constraints, we were unable
to evaluate performance with the larger document
sets and the larger models.
