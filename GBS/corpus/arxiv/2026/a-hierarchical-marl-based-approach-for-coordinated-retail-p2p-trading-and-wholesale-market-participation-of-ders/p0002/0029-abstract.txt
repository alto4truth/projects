3.2. Hierarchical DRL-Based Stackelberg Game
Framework
A hierarchical DRL framework, i.e., SEAM-LESS, is
proposed under a Stackelberg game setup to solve the
Markov game, as shown in Figure 2. The aggregator agent,
as the game leader, sets price signals to define its wholesale
and retail market policies, while the prosumer agents, as the
game followers, learn local control policies to govern their
decision-making in the retail market.
