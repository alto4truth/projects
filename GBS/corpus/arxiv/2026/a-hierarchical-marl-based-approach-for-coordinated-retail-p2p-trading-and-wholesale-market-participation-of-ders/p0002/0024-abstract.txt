After the P2P market clears, settled prices and quantities
are allocated among participants, with any unmatched bids
and/or offers tracked as residuals. Once all 24 hours’ P2P
transactions are processed, the aggregator compiles hourly
residuals and transacts with the wholesale market or utility
on behalf of prosumers, aiming to optimize its profit (30).
Following wholesale settlements, the aggregator distributes
net profits or costs back to the P2P participants. This approach accommodates the single round 24-hour day-ahead
wholesale market while allowing hourly price-setting between the aggregator and local P2P participants.
