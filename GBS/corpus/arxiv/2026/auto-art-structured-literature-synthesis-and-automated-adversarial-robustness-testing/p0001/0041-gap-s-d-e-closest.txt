Threats to validity.Internal validity.Pre-screening gate
thresholds ( τmask = 0.1 , WB/BB gap 0.15) were tuned on
the RobustBench CIFAR-10 leaderboard; these may require
recalibration for different data distributions, model families, or
perturbation budgets. The ablation study (Table 9) mitigates
this by showing robustness across five random seeds, but crossdataset validation remains future work.
External validity.Our empirical evaluation uses CIFAR-10
models exclusively. While CIFAR-10 is the standard RobustBench benchmark, generalisability to ImageNet-scale models,
multimodal architectures, and production LLMs is not yet
demonstrated. The structured synthesis is bounded by nine
corpus sources; future systematic reviews with larger corpora
may reveal additional patterns or contradict our gap rankings.
Construct validity.Kendall τ between RDI and AutoAttack
rankings measures ordinal agreement but not calibration—
a high τ does not guarantee that RDI absolute values are
meaningful thresholds for deployment decisions.
