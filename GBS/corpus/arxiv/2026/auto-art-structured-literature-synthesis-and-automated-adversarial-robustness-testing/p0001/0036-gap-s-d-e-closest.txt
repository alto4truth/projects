Edge-case checklist:
• Data leakage:Calibration set disjoint from evaluation set;
calibration uses held-out RobustBench entries not in the
main evaluation.
• Compute:RDI computation on ViT-B/16 requires ∼2 GB
VRAM for 500 samples; LLM RDI on 7B models requires
∼14 GB (single A100). Calibration fitting is trivial (<1 s).
• Reproducibility:Layer selection heuristic (which layer’s
features to use), centroid computation for imbalanced
classes, isotonic regression implementation.
• Baselines:Raw RDI [ 43], Clever score [ 54], Lin region
counting.
• Ablation variables:(i) layer choice (penultimate vs. multilayer aggregation), (ii) calibration method (isotonic vs. Platt
scaling vs. temperature), (iii) number of calibration models
(5, 10, 20).
