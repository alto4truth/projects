6 Methodology Audit
Step 2: Synthesis.Dominant methodology:Computational
ML experiments and benchmark construction account for 7 of
9 entries. Rationale: executable attacks operationalise claims
where analytic certification is prohibitive [C5], and reproducibility demands public code and checkpoints [C1,C2,C6].
Absent despite relevance:Randomised controlled trials
(RCTs) of deployment pipelines, ethnography of security practitioner workflows, prospective field studies of adversarial
incidents in production, and user studies of how practitioners
interpret robustness reports. These absences limit the field’s
ability to validate that lab findings transfer to operational settings.
