12 Experimental Evaluation
We validate AUTO-ART’s three core claims: (1) pre-screening
gates improve evaluation efficiency and reliability, (2) multinorm evaluation exposes hidden worst-case failures, and
(3) the framework’s compliance and testing infrastructure
meets production standards.
12.1 Experimental Setup
Models.We evaluate ten models from the RobustBench
CIFAR-10 ℓ∞ (ε= 8/255) leaderboard [14], spanning stateof-the-art (Wang2023Better, 70.69% robust acc.) to baseline
(Wong2020Fast, 43.21%).
Attack configuration.Default parameters: ε= 0.031
(8/255), step size η= 0.007 (2/255), 100 PGD iterations, batch size 32. Multi-norm epsilons: ℓ∞ ∈
{0.01,0.03,0.05,0.1,0.3} , ℓ2 ∈ {0.1,0.3,0.5,1.0,2.0} ,
ℓ1 ∈ {1.0,3.0,5.0,10.0} . Attacks per norm follow Table 5:
ℓ∞: FGSM, PGD, AutoPGD;ℓ 2: C&W, DeepFool;ℓ 1: EAD.
Pre-screening parameters.FOSC threshold τmask = 0.1;
white-box/black-box discrepancy threshold 0.15; noise σ=
0.01with 10 samples; RDI computed on 500 samples.
