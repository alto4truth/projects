12.5 Experiment 4: Ablation Study
We ablate the three core components of AUTO-ART’s prescreening gate to isolate their individual contributions (Table 9).
Key findings: (1) Removing RDI collapses the speedup
from 30×to 1.2×while preserving detection rate, confirming
RDI is the efficiency engine. (2) Removing FOSC drops
masking detection from 92% to 67%, confirming FOSC is the
primary masking signal. (3) The three-signal ensemble (FOSC
+ RDI + WB/BB discrepancy) outperforms any single signal
on both ranking fidelity (τ) and detection rate, justifying the
multi-signal design.
