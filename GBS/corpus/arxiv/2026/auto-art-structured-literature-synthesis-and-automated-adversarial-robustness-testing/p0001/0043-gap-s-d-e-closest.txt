15 Conclusion
This work presents a structured literature synthesis and the
AUTO-ART framework. The analysis identifies three intellectual threads (reliable evaluation, gradient masking, multiattack threats), five ranked gaps with explicit resolution paths,
eight untested assumptions ranked by consequence severity,
and a unifying open question:what evaluation stack should be
mandatory before certifying a model as safe against adaptive,
multi-channel adversaries?
On the empirical side, AUTO-ART’s pre-screening gate detects gradient masking in 92% of flagged configurations, RDI
