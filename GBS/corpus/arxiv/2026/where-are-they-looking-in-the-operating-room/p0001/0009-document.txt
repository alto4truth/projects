Our contributions can be summarized as follows: (1) we
highlight the clinical importance of studying gaze-following
in the OR for surgical workflow analysis, and extend two
existing external OR datasets with gaze annotations; (2) we
present a transformer-based approach that processes generated
gaze heatmaps solely to address clinical role prediction and
surgical phase recognition on the 4D-OR dataset, and reach a
performance on par with the semantic scene graph-based approaches; (3) we extend the Team-OR dataset with a new activity label, propose a novel self-supervised learning strategy
to train a spatial-temporal model that encodes clip-level gaze
features, and achieve state-of-the-art performance on the team
communication detection task.
