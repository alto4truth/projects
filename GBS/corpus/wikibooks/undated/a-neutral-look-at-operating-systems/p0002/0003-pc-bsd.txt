== Further reading ==
"A Neutral Look at Operating Systems" generally looks at OSes designed for desktop and laptop personal computers, which are less than 2% of all the computers in the world. For OSes designed to run on the other 98% of all the computers in the world, see Embedded Systems/Common RTOS and Embedded Control Systems Design/Operating systems.
