=== Program counter indirect with index ===
Assembler syntax:

x(PC,Dn.W)
x(PC,Dn.W*scale)
x(PC,Dn.L)
x(PC,Dn.L*scale)
x(PC,An.W)
x(PC,An.W*scale)
x(PC,An.L)
x(PC,An.L*scale)
(x)(PC,Dn.W)
(x)(PC,Dn.W*scale)
(x)(PC,Dn.L)
(x)(PC,Dn.L*scale)
(x)(PC,An.W)
(x)(PC,An.W*scale)
(x)(PC,An.L)
(x)(PC,An.L*scale)
(x,PC,Dn.W)
(x,PC,Dn.W*scale)
(x,PC,Dn.L)
(x,PC,Dn.L*scale)
(x,PC,An.W)
(x,PC,An.W*scale)
(x,PC,An.L)
(x,PC,An.L*scale)
Like PC with displacement, but another register is added as well. Scale can be 1, 2, 4, or 8. Scale is not supported on all devices. Some assemblers won't take certain syntaxes.
