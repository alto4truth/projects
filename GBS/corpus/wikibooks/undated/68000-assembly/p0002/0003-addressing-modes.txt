=== Indirect addressing with postincrement ===
Assembler syntax:

(An)+
Same as indirect addressing, but An will be increased by the size of the operation after the instruction is executed. The only exception is byte operations on A7 - this register must point to an even address, so it will always increment by at least 2.
Example:

Copies to D0 the longword to which A1 points, and increases A1 with 4 (because of Long).
