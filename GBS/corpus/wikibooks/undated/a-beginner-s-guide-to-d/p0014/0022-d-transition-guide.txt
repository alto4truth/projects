=== Arrays (and more) ===

We use int[], as opposed to int ages[] or int* ages or int *ages. Arrays are proper types in D, not just aliases for pointers. Those familiar with scripting languages like Perl and Python should find this much more comfortable. For the rest of you, wait a few minutes and then you'll appreciate it.
