== Converting the frequency table to proper CSV ==
The output of uniq -c is not in proper CSV form. This makes is necessary to convert the output if further operations on the output are wanted.
Here we use a bit of inline perl to rewrite the lines and reverse the order of the fields.

$ cut -d" " -f2 gender.txt | sort | uniq -c | perl -pe 's/^\s*([0-9]+) (\S+).*/$2, $1/'
F, 3966
M, 1051
