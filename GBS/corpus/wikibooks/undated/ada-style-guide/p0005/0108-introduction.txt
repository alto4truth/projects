== Typographic Conventions ==
This style guide uses the following typographic conventions:

Serif font
General presentation of information.
Italicized serif font
Publication titles and emphasis.
Boldfaced serif font
Section headings.
Boldfaced sans serif font
Subheadings for guideline, instantiation, example, rationale, notes, exceptions, automation notes, caution, and subheadings in Summary sections.
Typewriter font:
Syntax of code.
