Construct a complete binary tree.
A complete binary tree with height H is defined as follows:
The levels 1,2,3,...,H-1 contain the maximum number of nodes.
In level H, which may contain less than the maximum possible number of nodes, all the nodes are "left-adjusted". This means that in a level-order tree traversal all internal nodes come first, the leaves come second, and empty successors come last.
