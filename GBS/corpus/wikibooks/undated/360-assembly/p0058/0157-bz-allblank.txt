Example testing a packed field.

SR 2,2
TRT PACKDATA,TRTPACK TEST THE PACKED FIELD WITH 'C', 'D', OR 'F' SIGN
BC 2,GOODDATA IF ANY SIGN '0A' - '0F' IS GOOD, USE THIS.
B BRTBL(2) TO RESTRICT SIGNS TO '0C' '0D' AND '0F', USE THIS.
