# Film TLDR: "The Shawshank Redemption"

**Genre:** Drama  
**Year:** 1994  
**Runtime:** 142 minutes

**TLDR:** Banker Andy Dufresne is wrongfully imprisoned for murder. Over 19 years, he befriends fellow inmate Red and eventually escapes using a rock hammer and careful planning. Meanwhile, he helps the prison guards with financial matters and transforms the library. The film explores themes of hope, friendship, and redemption.

**Key Moments:** Andy escapes through a sewage pipe during a storm; Red's parole hearing; the final reunion in Mexico.

**Why It Matters:** Considered one of the greatest films ever made, it's a masterclass in character development and storytelling that proves hope can survive even in the darkest places.
