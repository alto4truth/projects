# Film TLDR: "Inception"

**Genre:** Sci-Fi/Thriller  
**Year:** 2010  
**Runtime:** 148 minutes

**TLDR:** Dom Cobb specializes in extracting secrets through dream-sharing technology. He's hired for the inverse task - planting an idea in someone's mind (inception). The team enters multiple layers of dreams to plant an idea in Robert Fischer's mind. The mission succeeds, but Cobb must confront his guilt over his wife's death.

**Key Moments:** The rotating hallway fight; the snow fortress sequence; Cobb spinning the top at the end.

**Why It Matters:** A mind-bending exploration of reality, guilt, and the power of ideas. Christopher Nolan's complex narrative structure rewards multiple viewings.
