# Film TLDR: "Titanic"

**Genre:** Romance/Drama  
**Year:** 1997  
**Runtime:** 195 minutes

**TLDR:** A first-class passenger and a lower-class artist fall in love aboard the RMS Titanic. Their romance unfolds against the backdrop of the ship's doomed maiden voyage, culminating in tragedy when the "unsinkable" ship hits an iceberg and sinks.

**Key Moments:** The ship hitting the iceberg; Jack saving Rose; the final scene with the elderly Rose; the ship breaking in half.

**Why It Matters:** A sweeping romance that became the highest-grossing film of its time, proving that epic love stories still resonate with audiences worldwide.
