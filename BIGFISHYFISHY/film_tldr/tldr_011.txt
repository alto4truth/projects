# Film TLDR: "The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring"

**Genre:** Fantasy/Adventure  
**Year:** 2001  
**Runtime:** 178 minutes

**TLDR:** Frodo Baggins inherits a powerful ring from his uncle Bilbo and must destroy it in Mount Doom to save Middle-earth from the Dark Lord Sauron. He forms the Fellowship of the Ring with eight companions including Gandalf, Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli.

**Key Moments:** The ring's discovery; Gandalf's fall in Moria; the Fellowship's formation; Boromir's redemption; Frodo's separation from the group.

**Why It Matters:** Peter Jackson's epic adaptation revolutionized fantasy cinema with groundbreaking practical effects, sweeping cinematography, and emotional depth that honored Tolkien's masterpiece.
