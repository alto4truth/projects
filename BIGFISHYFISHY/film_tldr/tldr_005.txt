# Film TLDR: "The Dark Knight"

**Genre:** Superhero/Crime  
**Year:** 2008  
**Runtime:** 152 minutes

**TLDR:** Batman faces his greatest challenge yet when the Joker wreaks havoc on Gotham City. The Joker's chaos forces Batman to question his methods and morality, while Harvey Dent becomes Gotham's new hero until tragedy transforms him into Two-Face.

**Key Moments:** The bank heist opening; Joker's truck hijacking; the ferry scene; Batman's sacrifice of his reputation.

**Why It Matters:** Elevated the superhero genre with its complex themes of heroism, sacrifice, and the thin line between order and chaos. Heath Ledger's Joker performance became legendary.
