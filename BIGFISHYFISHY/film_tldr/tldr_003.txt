# Film TLDR: "The Godfather"

**Genre:** Crime/Drama  
**Year:** 1972  
**Runtime:** 175 minutes

**TLDR:** The aging patriarch of a crime dynasty transfers control to his reluctant son. Michael Corleone transforms from war hero to ruthless mob boss as he protects the family business and seeks power. The film explores themes of loyalty, family, and the cost of ambition.

**Key Moments:** The horse head scene; Michael's baptism while rivals are killed; the final scene showing Michael's complete transformation.

**Why It Matters:** Considered one of the greatest films ever made, it's a masterclass in storytelling, character development, and the exploration of American power structures.
