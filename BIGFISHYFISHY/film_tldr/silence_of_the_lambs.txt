# Film TLDR: "The Silence of the Lambs"

**Genre:** Thriller/Crime  
**Year:** 1991  
**Runtime:** 118 minutes

**TLDR:** FBI trainee Clarice Starling seeks help from imprisoned cannibal Dr. Hannibal Lecter to catch serial killer Buffalo Bill. As she delves deeper into the investigation, she confronts her own psychological demons while getting closer to the truth.

**Key Moments:** Clarice's first meeting with Lecter; the "quid pro quo" scene; Buffalo Bill's house; Lecter's escape; the final confrontation.

**Why It Matters:** A masterclass in psychological thriller filmmaking that explores the thin line between predator and prey, sanity and madness, while delivering unforgettable performances from Jodie Foster and Anthony Hopkins.
