# Film TLDR: "Goodfellas"

**Genre:** Crime/Drama  
**Year:** 1990  
**Runtime:** 146 minutes

**TLDR:** Henry Hill grows up in the mob, working his way up from small-time criminal to major player in the Lucchese crime family. The film chronicles his rise and fall, including his involvement in the Lufthansa heist and his eventual cooperation with the FBI.

**Key Moments:** The "funny how?" scene; the Copacabana sequence; the murder of Billy Batts; Henry's nervous breakdown; the final car scene.

**Why It Matters:** Scorsese's masterpiece redefined the crime genre with its kinetic energy, moral complexity, and authentic portrayal of organized crime's seductive and destructive nature.
