# Film TLDR: "Jurassic Park"

**Genre:** Sci-Fi/Adventure  
**Year:** 1993  
**Runtime:** 127 minutes

**TLDR:** A billionaire creates a dinosaur theme park using cloned DNA, but the dinosaurs escape when the park's security systems fail. Visitors and staff must survive while evading deadly prehistoric creatures to reach safety.

**Key Moments:** The T-Rex reveal; the kitchen scene; the raptors in the visitor center; the final helicopter escape.

**Why It Matters:** Revolutionized CGI and special effects while delivering thrilling adventure that captured the imagination of multiple generations.
