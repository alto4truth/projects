# Film TLDR: "The Matrix"

**Genre:** Sci-Fi/Action  
**Year:** 1999  
**Runtime:** 136 minutes

**TLDR:** Computer programmer Thomas Anderson discovers reality is a simulation controlled by machines. He joins a rebellion led by Morpheus to free humanity from their digital prison. Neo becomes "The One" who can manipulate the Matrix and potentially save the human race.

**Key Moments:** The red pill/blue pill choice; the bullet-dodging scene; Neo's awakening; the final confrontation with Agent Smith.

**Why It Matters:** Revolutionized action cinema with its "bullet time" effects and philosophical questions about reality, choice, and what it means to be human.
