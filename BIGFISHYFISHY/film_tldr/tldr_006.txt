# Film TLDR: "Forrest Gump"

**Genre:** Drama/Romance  
**Year:** 1994  
**Runtime:** 142 minutes

**TLDR:** A simple man with a pure heart witnesses and influences major historical events while searching for his lost love Jenny. Forrest accidentally becomes a war hero, ping-pong champion, and businessman, but his greatest achievement is his unwavering love and loyalty.

**Key Moments:** Forrest running across America; his meeting with President Kennedy; the bench scenes with Jenny; the revelation about his son.

**Why It Matters:** A heartwarming tale about the power of simplicity and goodness in a complex world, proving that ordinary people can have extraordinary impact.
