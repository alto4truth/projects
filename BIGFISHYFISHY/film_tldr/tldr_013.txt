# Film TLDR: "Fight Club"

**Genre:** Drama/Thriller  
**Year:** 1999  
**Runtime:** 139 minutes

**TLDR:** An unnamed narrator suffering from insomnia meets Tyler Durden, a charismatic soap salesman. Together they form an underground fight club that evolves into an anarchist organization. The twist reveals Tyler is the narrator's split personality.

**Key Moments:** The first fight; Marla Singer's introduction; the fight club rules; Tyler's philosophy; the revelation of the twist; the final scene with the narrator and Marla.

**Why It Matters:** A darkly comic critique of consumerism and masculinity that became a cultural phenomenon, inspiring everything from fashion trends to philosophical discussions about identity and rebellion.
