# Film TLDR: "Casablanca"

**Genre:** Romance/Drama  
**Year:** 1942  
**Runtime:** 102 minutes

**TLDR:** Rick Blaine, a cynical American expatriate running a nightclub in Casablanca during World War II, is reunited with his former lover Ilsa Lund. She arrives with her husband, a Czech resistance leader, seeking exit visas. Rick must choose between love and duty.

**Key Moments:** "Here's looking at you, kid"; the piano scene; the airport farewell; Rick's speech about the problems of three people; the final scene with Rick and Ilsa.

**Why It Matters:** A timeless classic that epitomizes Hollywood's golden age, featuring some of cinema's most memorable dialogue and Humphrey Bogart's iconic performance as the reluctant hero.
