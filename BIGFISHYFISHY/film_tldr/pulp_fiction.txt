# Film TLDR: "Pulp Fiction"

**Genre:** Crime/Black Comedy  
**Year:** 1994  
**Runtime:** 154 minutes

**TLDR:** Multiple interconnected stories involving hitmen, boxers, gangsters, and their wives unfold in non-linear fashion. Vincent Vega and Jules Winnfield carry out a mission for their boss Marsellus Wallace, while other characters navigate their own moral dilemmas.

**Key Moments:** The briefcase mystery; Vincent's dance scene; the diner robbery; Butch's decision to return the watch.

**Why It Matters:** Tarantino's breakthrough redefined independent cinema with its nonlinear narrative, pop culture references, and exploration of morality and redemption.
