# Film TLDR: "Star Wars: A New Hope"

**Genre:** Sci-Fi/Adventure  
**Year:** 1977  
**Runtime:** 121 minutes

**TLDR:** Farm boy Luke Skywalker joins rebels to destroy the Empire's Death Star and rescue Princess Leia. With help from Obi-Wan Kenobi, Han Solo, and Chewbacca, Luke discovers his destiny as a Jedi Knight and helps restore hope to the galaxy.

**Key Moments:** The opening crawl; the trench run; Obi-Wan's sacrifice; the final celebration.

**Why It Matters:** Launched one of cinema's most beloved franchises and redefined the sci-fi genre with its blend of mythology, adventure, and groundbreaking special effects.
