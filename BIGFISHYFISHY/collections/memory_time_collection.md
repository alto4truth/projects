# Stories Collection: Memory & Time

This collection explores themes of memory, time, and the connections between past, present, and future.

## Stories in this collection:

1. **The Clockmaker's Secret** (story_2.txt)
   - A clockmaker who can hear time's whispers discovers a backwards world and must save the world from eternal stillness.

2. **The Memory Merchant** (story_5.txt)
   - A shopkeeper who trades in memories faces an impossible choice when his lost love enters his shop.

3. **The Cartographer of Lost Places** (story_6.txt)
   - A woman who maps places that no longer exist discovers she's mapping her own past.

4. **The Night Shift at the Museum of Time** (story_7.txt)
   - A museum guard discovers his calling as guardian of time itself through a temporal mirror.

5. **The Lighthouse Keeper's Daughter** (story_9.txt)
   - A young woman with temporal vision must choose between self-preservation and protecting others.

## Themes explored:
- The weight and value of memory
- Time as both linear and cyclical
- The geography of loss and remembrance
- Temporal responsibility and sacrifice
- The intersection of past and future

## Reading order:
These stories can be read in any order, but reading them chronologically (2→5→6→7→9) creates a narrative arc from individual memory to cosmic responsibility.
