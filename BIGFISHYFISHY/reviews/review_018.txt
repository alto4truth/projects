# Review: "The Lighthouse Keeper"

**Rating: 5/5 stars**

This atmospheric period piece follows a lighthouse keeper who discovers a mysterious shipwreck survivor with no memory of her past. The film builds mystery and tension through masterful use of isolation and natural elements.

The cinematography is stunning, with the lighthouse and surrounding sea serving as both setting and character. The two-hander performances are exceptional, creating a compelling dynamic between the characters.

**Strengths:**
- Exceptional cinematography and atmosphere
- Strong, nuanced performances
- Masterful build-up of tension and mystery
- Beautiful use of natural elements

**Weaknesses:**
- May be too slow-paced for some viewers

**Verdict:** A beautifully crafted mystery that rewards patient viewers. Essential viewing for fans of atmospheric, character-driven cinema.
