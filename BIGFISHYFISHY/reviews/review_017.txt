# Review: "Digital Ghosts"

**Rating: 4/5 stars**

This horror film explores what happens when people start receiving messages from deceased loved ones through social media algorithms. The concept is genuinely unsettling and taps into contemporary fears about technology and grief.

The film uses found footage and social media interfaces to create an authentic, modern horror experience. The tension builds gradually, relying on psychological horror rather than cheap scares.

**Strengths:**
- Innovative and timely horror concept
- Effective use of modern technology as horror device
- Strong atmospheric tension
- Authentic portrayal of social media culture

**Weaknesses:**
- Some may find the concept too close to home
- Limited character development

**Verdict:** A clever, contemporary horror film that uses familiar technology to create genuine unease. Perfect for viewers who appreciate horror with social commentary.
