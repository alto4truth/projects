# Review: "The Last Bookstore"

**Rating: 5/5 stars**

This love letter to literature follows the owner of a struggling independent bookstore as she fights to keep her shop alive in the age of digital media. The film celebrates the tactile experience of reading while acknowledging the challenges facing traditional bookstores.

The dialogue sparkles with wit and intelligence, and the supporting cast brings depth to every character. The cinematography captures the cozy, intimate atmosphere of a well-loved bookstore.

**Strengths:**
- Genuine love for literature and reading
- Well-drawn characters and relationships
- Warm, inviting visual style

**Weaknesses:**
- Some may find the subject matter too niche

**Verdict:** A heartwarming celebration of books and community that should resonate with anyone who has ever found solace in a good story. Essential viewing for book lovers.
