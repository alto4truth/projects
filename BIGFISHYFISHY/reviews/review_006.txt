# Review: "Midnight Radio"

**Rating: 4.5/5 stars**

This intimate character study follows a late-night radio host who takes calls from strangers across the city. Each caller reveals a piece of the urban puzzle, creating a mosaic of contemporary life.

The film relies heavily on voice work and audio design, creating a unique cinematic experience. The lead performance is subtle and compelling, conveying emotion through vocal nuance alone.

**Strengths:**
- Innovative use of sound and voice
- Strong ensemble of voice actors
- Authentic portrayal of urban isolation and connection

**Weaknesses:**
- May not appeal to viewers who prefer visual storytelling

**Verdict:** A refreshingly original film that proves compelling stories can be told without traditional visual elements. A must-see for fans of character-driven narratives.
