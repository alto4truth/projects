# Review: "The Garden of Echoes"

**Rating: 4/5 stars**

This magical realism drama follows a woman who can hear the memories of plants in her grandmother's garden. The concept is whimsical yet profound, exploring themes of inheritance, memory, and the connections between generations.

The cinematography brings the botanical world to life with stunning close-ups and natural lighting. The lead performance is grounded and authentic, making the fantastical elements feel believable.

**Strengths:**
- Unique and imaginative premise
- Beautiful visual style
- Strong emotional core

**Weaknesses:**
- Some scenes feel overly long
- Supporting characters could be more developed

**Verdict:** A charming and thoughtful film that celebrates the wisdom of nature and the power of family bonds. Perfect for viewers who enjoy gentle, contemplative cinema.
